Sirețel Cycloroute

Description

It is a very beautiful route dedicated even to families with children over 10 years old which starts in front of Siretel City Hall and aims to promote the objectives of natural and man-made heritage within the Sireţel commune, especially Sireţel villages, Satu Nou, Humosu and Berezlogi. The natural setting is one with natural potential that it also includes a beautiful deciduous forest and the highest altitudes in Iasi County (approx. 600 m). Starting in front of the city hall we follow the asphalt road that leads us through traditional houses but also numerous crosses to Satu Nou and Humosu respectively. Cycloroute climb a steady slope to kilometer 11 and then return to a ridge mainly through the forest / at the edge of the forest to the village of Berezlogi. From this mane we have more many points of perspective on the forest in the area towards Lake Pârcovaci. It is worth mentioning and the fact that it is one of the few places in Iasi County where we can admire micro wind power plants.

Total length: 25 km
Vertical climb: 310 m
Maximum altitude: 528 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes up to 7%)
Road: DC (partly asphalt, gravel), agricultural and forestry road
Degree of difficulty: Medium;
Recommended bike type: Mountain bike / Trekking;
Cyclist profile: Trained amateur/ Families with children/ Children +10 years
Journey time: 3 1/2- 4 h;
