Fântânele Cycloroute

Description

The cycling route is a little more hilly (410m elevation difference) given its positioning geographical area of Fântânele commune. The starting point is the village center Fântânele (Fântânele City Hall). After refueling at the grocery store (point hydration) from the village and visiting the Orthodox Church, the route goes through the streets perpendicular, specific to the village Fântânele/ Cohornele Caprei. The trail winds on village streets, then descend to the grazing area of the village, then climb a slope to the Fântânele forest, admiring on the right side a first sheepfold. Once Arriving at the edge of the forest, we can make a stop admiring the Moldavian village throughout his splendor. We follow the cyclotrail at the edge of the forest and then exit to the summit between the villages of Fântânele and Arama among the cereal fields. Then the road descends on the left side towards the entrance to the village from where you can admire the Catholic Church, as well as and a traditional sheepfold and goat sheepfold covered with reeds and fenced with grapes, after which descends to the right on a lane that leads us to the canals of the village, the place where they can be Seen flocks of ducks and geese of villagers. We cross the channel then climb to the side straight, past another sheepfold and a fountain with a watershed, to the forest of the Crow, after which we return on a ridge to the center of the village. This high grain area offers A beautiful view of the village and surroundings. It is worth mentioning that The cycloroute can be divided into two segments.

Route length: 25 km
Vertical climb: 410 m
Maximum altitude: 171 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes up to 12%)
Road: DJ, DC (partly asphalt, gravel), farm roads
Degree of difficulty: Medium - Difficult;
Recommended bike type: Mountain bike / Trekking;
Cyclist profile: Trained amateur/ Families with children/ Children +13 years
Journey time: 3 1/2 - 4 hours;
