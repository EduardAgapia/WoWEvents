Focuri Cycloroute

Description

The cycling route aims to highlight to tourists the main attractions of the commune Fires, starting from the center of Focuri village (Focuri City Hall). After food at the grocery store (hydration point) and visiting the Orthodox Church, The route goes through the streets of the Lipovan community in the area. The first stop will be at The Lipovan Church, the pride of the Lipovan inhabitants. About the Lipovan community from Focuri we can also find out by visiting the Ethnographic Museum in the center of the village. They can be admired traditional Lipovan houses and not only, historical testimonies of the coexistence of several communities in these lands. Then, the trail winds through the old streets of the village to the Catholic Church, and after a gentle climb on the unpaved street of the village descend to the pond on the outskirts of the village, which is part of the Eleste chain of Jijia and Miletin. Here you can admire herds of cows and sheep. The road follows the bank of the pond to the junction of the main road, after which we follow the road asphalted for about 2 km. In this section, birds, specific vegetation Ponds and herds of cattle can be admired in all their splendor. Then we follow agricultural road another 2-3 km winding right into a beautiful and quiet valley to the tourist sheepfold covered with hanu lupilor reeds. After a well-deserved stop at Sheepfold for hydration, the cycloroute climbs a steep slope behind the sheepfold, crossing cereal fields, up to the Fantanele forest, after which they descend to the village where they can be Other traditional houses inscribed with various folk motifs are also admired. Last The slope takes us to the center of the commune, Focuri City Hall, the final point of the cycloroute our.

Route length: 27 km
Vertical climb: 270 m
Maximum altitude: 168 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes of over 10%)
Road: DJ, DC (partly asphalt, gravel), farm roads
Degree of difficulty: Medium;
Recommended bike type: Mountain bike / Trekking;
Cyclist profile: Trained amateur/ Families with children/ Children +12 years
Journey time: 3-3 1/2 hours;
