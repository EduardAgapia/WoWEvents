Erbiceni Cycloroute

Description

The cycling route within Erbiceni commune aims to unite the 5 component villages: Erbiceni, Spinoasa, Bârleşti, Totoeşti and Sprânceana. The starting point of the route is constitutes the Erbiceni City Hall. After a short refueling at the grocery store (point hydration), the route directs us to the Sports Base crossing a small forest villages and some streets. We pass by the village mill, then, winding through the streets and Admiring some traditional houses and the village church, the road leads us to the village Thorny. At the entrance to the village, we pass through the area by two local producers (greenhouses of vegetables and fruit orchard), after which we head to the Orthodox church in Spinoasa and the building of the former Spinoasa Research Station (disused). We go back until the first intersection and turn right towards Spinoasa hall, where the ruins of a beautiful buildings. Then we follow the route to the ruins of the largest military site started by the Romanian Army in Iasi County (huge underground warehouse) near the forest Chicerea, after which we follow the route to Sprânceana village, a small Catholic community with A few well-preserved traditional houses and a more recent church. After a small hydration break at the village store, we head to Erbiceni hall, to continue the incursion towards the village of Bârleşti. After a steep slope we reach the village Totoești, where we reach the maximum altitude of the cycloroute (144 m), where we can admire a few peasant farms with typical architecture of this area, then we descend to the Lake Totoeşti, not before making a small stop at the Village Orthodox Church. Lake, of appreciable dimensions, is leased to a fishery, where they can be Several species of birds were observed, but also the herd of village cows. At the same time, in Totoești village can also stop at the local producer/processor Andrei's Farm, where you can taste some smoked meat specialties. The cycling route continues with southern transit of the village and return to the starting point of the cycling route (Erbiceni City Hall).

Route length: 25 km
Vertical climb: 260 m
Maximum altitude: 147 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes up to 8%)
Road: DJ, DC (partly asphalt, gravel), farm roads
Degree of difficulty: Easy - Medium;
Recommended bike type: Mountain bike / Trekking;
Cyclist profile: Trained amateur/ Families with children/ Children +10 years
Journey time: 3-3 1/2 hours;
