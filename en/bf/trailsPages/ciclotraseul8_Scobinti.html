Scobinți Cycloroute

Description

The cycling route in Scobinţi aims to highlight the natural heritage of cyclists and anthropogenically penetrated components within the Scobinţi commune: Feteşti, Scobinţi, Glassware and Poiana Mărului. The microzone is located near the Basaraba forest and Sangeap Basaraba Monastery which greatly increase its tourist attractiveness. Departure is makes in front of Scobinţi City Hall, after filling up from shops in the center of the village Fetesti. For less trained cyclists, due to the longer length of cycloroute, it is recommended to follow the short route: Scobinţi – Sangeap Monastery Basaraba – Scobinţi, by avoiding the community of Glassware. The first segment leads us from the center of the village on the old streets of the village after which we follow the main road that takes us to the edge of Basaraba forest towards the village of Glassria. The climb up here is It proves to be the most demanding part of this route. From the first intersection we do left to the domain of Sangeap Basaraba Monastery, a location located on top of the hill wooded. We return on the same road to the intersection and the cycloroute leads us to Glasseria village, one of the most interesting rural communities in Iasi, with many houses traditional well preserved. Once we arrive at the church in the center of the village we descend left towards the village museum, where Mr. Constantin, living human treasure, will welcome us. After listening to the beautiful stories of Mr. Constantine and visiting the museum of the village, we continue our route among traditional houses that are more or less beautiful, then we continue to Poiana Mărului village, from the edge of which we can enjoy The view with a beautiful panorama of the surroundings. From here, we descend to the village Hollows on old streets and even paths to the accumulation on the outskirts of the village Scobinţi, where we can also admire a traditional sheepfold. From here, we continue last kilometer of our cycling route on asphalt road to Scobinţi City Hall, point final.

Total length: 18 km
Vertical climb: 330 m
Maximum altitude: 410 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes up to 8%)
Road: DC (partly asphalt, gravel), farm roads
Degree of difficulty: Medium- Difficult;
Recommended bike type: Mountain bike / Trekking;
Cyclist profile: Trained amateur/ Families with children/ Children +12 years
Travel time: 4-4 1/2;
