Cycloroute of the Belcești-Focuri microregion

Description

The cycloroute dedicated to the entire territory of the Belcești – Focuri Microregion LAG connects all 8 cycloroutes listed above dedicated to each ATU. This connects ATUs and highlights the main natural heritage objectives (lakes and ponds, forests, various landforms) and anthropogenic (authentic villages, houses traditional, churches of various ethnicities, anthropogenic dams, small village museums), isolated traditional communities, including living human treasure. Promote small producers local (traditional sheepfolds, fish farms, vegetable greenhouses, fruit orchards, goats, etc.) and has the role of completing the cyclist's experience during Journey. We mention that this cycloroute with a total length of 221km and 2660 m Level difference is dedicated to trained cyclists, and the journey time It varies between 2-3 days, depending on each person's training level. There are hardly any accommodation capacities (except for Sangeap Monastery – Basaraba, Glassware or Hârlău – in the immediate vicinity of the LAG), but the location of the tent is allowed along the entire route.

Total length: 221 km
Vertical climb: 2660 m
Maximum altitude: 500 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes up to 12%)
Road: DJ and DC (partly asphalt, gravel), logging roads and roads Forest
Degree of difficulty: Difficult;
Recommended bike type: Mountain bike / Trekking;
Cyclist profile: Trained amateur / Cyclists;
Duration of traveled time: 2-3 days;
