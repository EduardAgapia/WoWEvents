Sângeap Basaraba Monastery

Description

In memory of the old monastery Sângeap from 1399, destructed by the Soviet army in 1944, in 1997 it was decided to establish the Sângeap Hermitage in another place, on the Basaraba hill, in a clearing surrounded by forests. The wall church was to be dedicated to "The Spring of Healing" and "The Transfiguration". In 2000 it was decided to transform the hermitage into a monastery, and In 2003 the wooden church was built, dedicated to "The Protection of the Mother of God and "The Great Martyr" Mine". The wooden church was built in just 3 weeks by some craftsmen from Vatra Moldoviței at the expense of the director of S.C. Cotnari S.A., Constantin Deleanu. Works on the wall church, executed in the Stephenian style, they are ongoing.

Location

Scobinți, Sticlăria village
