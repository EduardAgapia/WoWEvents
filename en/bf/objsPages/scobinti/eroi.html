The monument to the heroes of the nation from Scobinţi

Description

The Heroes Monument in the park in front of Scobinți City Hall was raised in 2019 and was executed by the sculptor Constantin Crengăniș from Iasi. Statue the 3-meter range depicts a flag from the 52nd Infantry Regiment. This is equipped in campaign attire of the year 1916, has in his right hand the flag of battle of the regiment and in the left hand, in the right of the heart, an icon with the image of Jesus Christ. On his head he has an Adrian-type helmet, with which the army Romanian was equipped in the winter of 1916 and the spring of 1917. The statue is dedicated to the heroes of the nation who fell in the battles for the independence and unification of Romania.

Location

Scobinți, Scobinți village
