Lipovan Churches

Description

The first place of worship of the Lipovan Russians in Focuri was built in the years 1900-1910, it was made of wood and it was renovated on several occasions, the last time in 1992-1993. One year after the renovation, the church burns with all the icons and cult books in it, most likely because of some thieves, who wanted to no traces remain after bursting. On its place was built a new place, between 1998 and 2002, dedicated to the Holy Great Prophet Elijah the Thesvitean, celebrated on August 2. Smaller in size in this church is officiated during the winter services. The church dedicated to Saint Nicholas of summer (22 May). Built between 1980 and 1984 it is a sumptuous building; in it the services are held during the summer. In the village divided by sectors, both churches are located in the same courtyard in sector VI.

Location

Focuri, Focuri village
