Church of Slobozia

Description

The construction of the church, dedicated to the Nativity of the Mother of God, is in the form of cross, with lateral apses at the nave and the altar in the semicircle. Iconostasis of linden wood was made by Croitoru Ioan, and the painting of its icons it was executed in oil, in a realistic style, by the artist Vasile Vasiliu (1946). Between years 1997 - 2004, the church was refurbished with the help of priests Isaac Ioan and Muraru Ionuţ Daniel, and in 2007 took place the re-consecration, committed by His Grace Calinic Botoşăneanul, Bishop-Vicar of the Archdiocese of Iasi.

Location

Sirețel
