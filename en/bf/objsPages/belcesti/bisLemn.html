The wooden Church from Munteni village

Description

The old church in Munteni village was built in 1906, made of wood on a stone foundation by the craftsman Cionea. In the period 1912-1913 the porch and the bell tower were added, and in the same period the bells above the gate were raised in the bell tower. Later, at the end of 1930, the construction of a new church was initiated, whose foundation stone began to be laid only in 1933, through the contribution of Victor Iamandi. The wooden church was consolidated in 2006 and today it is in good condition.
