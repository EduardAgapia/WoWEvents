Church of the Saints Archangels Michael and Gabriel

Description

The church dedicated to "The Saints Archangels Michael and Gabriel" in the village of Ciorăni was built on the site of an existing church from 1774, around the years 1860 - 1862 by Aga Radu Manolache and his wife Elena and was built in the traditional Moldavian style. Over time, the place of worship was repaired and re-consecrated several times and the parish priest is Mihai Zaharia. The church has a feast house and a museum point.

Location

Belcești, Ciorăni village
