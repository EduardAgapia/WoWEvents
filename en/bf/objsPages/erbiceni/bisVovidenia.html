Vovidenia Church

Description

Vovidenia Church in Bârleşti village was built between 1862- 1864 and is dedicated to "The Entrance of the Mother of God in the Church". Founders the place of worship were Gheorghe Stânca and Lascarache Cantacuzino, and at their insistence, the church was finished and consecrated in 1864. It was built of brick, in the shape of a basilica slightly clubs at the ends of the Holy Shrine, having above the porch a turnclopotnite. In the following years the church reached a state of advanced degradation, and in 1994 the restoration works began: the ecumenical objective it was plastered on the outside in stone dust, and on the inside they used the ''ariciu'' technique. Interior paintings have been restored in the technique fresco between 2000-2007 by the painter Dănilă Gheorghe, and the iconostasis was restored with gold leaf. The church was re-consecrated in 2008.

Location

Erbiceni, Bârlești village
