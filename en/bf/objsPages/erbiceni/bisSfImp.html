"Saint Emperors Constantine and Helen" Church

Description

The church dedicated to "The Saint Emperors Constantine and Helen" was built between 1805-1865. This is the foundation of the boyars Luca, Lascăr and Constantin Paşcanu. Initially, it was thought of as a Roman Catholic church, especially by the founders, but the community village did not agree with it, most of the inhabitants being of the Orthodox rite. It was finally decided to serve the priests Orthodox Christians, exclusively. The edifice is built in an imitation of the Gothic style, in the form of sailing ship. In 1980 the church had its share of works of consolidation. On the inside, the mural painting was made in the period 1989-1994 by Prof. Univ. Dimitrei Gavrilean. Iconostasis and the icons on it were painted by the priestess Cornelia Merticariu.

Location

Erbiceni, Erbiceni village
