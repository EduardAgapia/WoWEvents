The Roman Catholic Church "The Descent of the Holy Spirit"

Description

The construction of the church, dedicated to "The Descent of the Holy Spirit", was started in 1935 and was completed eight years later, with the help of parishioners and parish priests Alfons Weber and Petru Martinescu. The consecration of the place of worship took place in 1943. In 2015, renovation and modernization of the church are started. On the site of this church there was, in the past, another church built in 1910. On June 8, 2019, the consecration of the altar of the church took place by the Bishop of Iasi, His Eminence Petru Gherghel.

Location

Bălțați, Bălțați village
