Church of the Nativity of the Mother of God

Description

The construction of the church, dedicated to "The Birth of the Mother of God", began in 1936 and was completed only in 1961, because the works were stopped during the Second World War and, subsequently, of the atheist communist regime. The church was built of stone and was consecrated in 1961 by His Eminence Iustin Moisescu, Metropolitan of Moldavia and Suceava at that time. In 1981, the painting works of the church were completed, which was re-consecrated. Between 1990 and 1995, the social house was built, and in 2001 the current parish house was built. Over time, renovation works of the church were carried out, especially on the outside.

Location

Bălțați, Valea Oilor village
