Mirosloveşti Cycloroute (eMTB)

Description

The cycling route aims to highlight the natural and anthropogenic heritage of two component villages in Miroslovești commune: Miroslovești and Sochi. Departure is from Holy Trinity Orthodox Church in Miroslovesti. After a small refueling from the village shop, we follow the trail On the main road to the town hall we also enjoy the specific local architecture of the houses, then turn right to climb to the highest peak in the area. The agricultural exploitation road also offers us a beautiful perspective over the village of Miroslovesti, but also over the meadow Moldova. Reaching the summit, after a small hydration break, we turn left on the forest road from edge of the Hungarian Forest. After the forest ends, we turn left and carefully descend to the forest patch above the village of Sochi, and among agricultural crops, we intersect with the road asphalted. The village of Sochi is one of the best preserved villages in the Codru Pașcanilor area, with a diversity of traditional houses and specific local architecture, with traditional sheepfolds and people Hospitable. We make another small stop at the village church dedicated to the Holy Archangels Michael and Gabriel, then, on the asphalt road we return to the village of Miroslovesti, not before making another small stop at the Monument of Heroes and the Church of Saints Constantine and Helen, as well as at the only one rural tourist establishment Grandma's House, a well rustic household restored and preserved by its owners

Route length: 14 km
Vertical climb: 240 m
Maximum altitude: 439 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes up to 8%)
Road: DJ, DC (partly asphalt, gravel), farm roads
Degree of difficulty: Medium;
Recommended bike type: eMTB;
Cyclist profile: Amateur/ Families with children/ Children +10 years
Travel time: 2 1/2;
