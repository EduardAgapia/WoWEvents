Tătăruși Cycloroute - eMTB

Description

The cycling route within Tătăruși commune aims to unite the component villages: Tătărus, Iorcani, Uda and Pietrosu. It is worth mentioning that this commune is extremely well administered by local public authorities. The starting point of the route is Tătăruși Park near the Cultural Home. After a short refueling at the grocery store (point hydration) from the center of the village, the route follows a small loop to see the headquarters of the Tătărus City Hall, Kindergarten, After School and Tătăruși Vocational School and Firefighters' Museum, rehabilitated investments through European funds. We head to St. Basil the Great Orthodox Church, then make another loop through the secondary streets to climb to the place called by the locals, La Cruce, from where you can admire a beautiful panorama over the commune (at the edge of the forest). Of Here, among the cereal fields, on agricultural exploitation roads, we begin a smooth but long climb to the border with Suceava County, then descend and then climb again to the Iorcani forest (Tatarus Nature Reserve). After passing the familiar place La Podul Mare, we head to the archeological site Palanca Fortification, then we cross DC208F and besides the place Nănășeni we do left to the center of Iorcani village (near St. Demetrius Church) and another small hydration stop at the village shop. Then, we head to the edge, on the valley of a stream we connect with the village of Uda passing by and a beautiful pond (privately owned). From here, the paved road leads us to the center of Tătărus village, the end point of the cycloroute

Total length: 27 km
Vertical climb: 370 m
Maximum altitude: 455 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes up to 12%-14%)
Road: DJ, DC (partly asphalt, gravel), agricultural and forestry roads
Degree of difficulty: Medium - Difficult;
Recommended bike type: eMTB
Cyclist profile: Trained amateur/ Families with children/ Children +12 years
Journey time: 4 - 4 1/2 hours;
