Cycloroute Mirosloveşti village

Description

The present cycling route is much easier, even if it is only 1 km less (13 km) than route no. 5 d edicat to electric bicycles, with a smaller level difference (140m elevation). For this reason, we recommend it both to children over 10 years old, to families with children, and to less trained cyclists. The cycling route aims to highlight mainly the natural and anthropogenic heritage of the two Mirosloveşti and Sochi villages. Departure is from Holy Orthodox Church Trinity of Mirosloveşti. After a small refueling from the village shop, we follow the route on the main road to the town hall and enjoy and with the specific local architecture of the houses, then turn left to connect on the secondary road with the village of Sochi. At this stage of the route we admire the vegetable gardens and cereal fields. The village of Sochi is one of the best preserved villages in the Codru Pașcanilor area, with a diversity of traditional houses and specific local architecture, with traditional sheepfolds and hospitable people. We make a small stop to taste the vegetables produced in the solariums of the small local vegetable producer I.I. Maftei Ioan) and after visiting the beautiful streets of Sochi, we return to the road main asphalt. We make another small stop at the village church dedicated to the Holy Archangels Michael and Gavril, after which, on the paved road we return to the village of Miroslovesti, not before make a small stop at the Heroes' Monument and the Church of Saints Constantine and Helen, as well as at the only rural tourist establishment Casa Bunicii, a well-restored and preserved rustic homestead its owners.

Total length: 14 km
Vertical climb: 140 m
Maximum altitude: 346 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes up to 8%)
Road: DJ, DC, trails and logging roads
Degree of difficulty: Easy;
Recommended bike type: Mountain bike / Trekking;
Cyclist profile: Amateur/ Families with children/ Children +10 years
Journey time: 2 1/2 - 3 hours;
