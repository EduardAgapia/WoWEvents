Moţca Cycloroute

Description

The proposed cycling route is one specific to the promotion of the Moldovan village that starts in the heart of the commune, headquarters of the Codru Pașcanilor LAG. After refueling from the grocery store in the immediate vicinity of the premises, climb 200m on DN28A and then turn right onto a street Secondary of the village to admire some traditional houses specific to the area, followed by a steep slope that leads us to the village imas and the forest of Codrii Pașcanilor. Before entering forest, you can admire the panorama over Moţca village and Biseric ii Orthodox Saint Niculae. The cycling trail then crosses the old-growth forest until it crosses DN28A again. On the left is The Soviet cemetery, and across the road behind the forest is Monastery of St. Theodora. We arrive on Pașcani plateau and urmeaz trail on the farm road on the edge of the Codru forest Pașcanilor. From here you can offer a beautiful perspective on Pașcani municipality. We cross again deciduous and pine forest, after which we follow the farm road, among the fields of grain, up to the paved road that leads us directly to the Boureni Hermitage. After visiting the hermitage, We go to the intersection in the center of the village to the hydration point (grocery store), after that we continue our way to Church of the Nativity of the Virgin Mary Among traditional old houses well preserved.Then, we follow Dragoș Vodă Street to St. Nicholas Church of the old rite, after which we cross the cereal fields on the outskirts of Boureni village until we have a panorama special over the Stanisoara Mountains. The last part of the cycling route is traveled on a grassy road to the center of Moţca village, passing by a traditional sheepfold, Moţca City Hall and up to the headquarters of the LAG, the end point of the route.

Trail length: 14 km
Vertical climb: 270 m
Maximum altitude: 450 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes of 14%)
Road: DN, DC (partly asphalt, gravel), agricultural roads, forest road
Degree of difficulty: Medium;
Recommended bike type: Mountain–bike electric (eMTB).
Cyclist profile: Amateur/ Families with children/ Children +12 years
Journey time: 3-3 1/2 hours.
