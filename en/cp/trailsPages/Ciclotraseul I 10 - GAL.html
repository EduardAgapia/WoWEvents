GAL Codrii Pașcanilor Cycloroute

Description

Cycling route dedicated to the entire territory of the LAG Codrii Pașcanilor connects all 9 cycloroutes listed above dedicated to each ATU (Moţca, Tătărus, Cristești, Mirosloveşti and Stolniceni-Prăjescu). It connects UATs and highlights the main natural heritage objectives (forests and nature reserves, rivers, ponds and ponds, different forms of relief) and anthropogenic (authentic villages, traditional houses, churches of various ethnicities, cemeteries, traditional sheepfolds, small village museums), isolated traditional communities (Sochi, Homita, Boureni). Promotes small local producers (sheepfolds traditional, vegetable greenhouses, goat farms, etc.) and has The role of completing the cyclist's experience during the trip. Please note that this Cycling route with a total length of 92 km and 1170 m elevation difference is dedicated only to their cyclists train, and the journey time varies between 1-2 days, depending on the level of training. Accommodation capacities exists (Moţca hunting lodge, Cristesti pension, Codru chalet Pașcanilor, Markos Pension or Grandma's House), and The placement of the tent is tolerated along the entire cycloroute. Among the main tourist attractions we mention: Valea Moldovei, Codrii Pașcanilor, The wooden church from Mitești, the traditional houses from Verseni, Sochi and Boureni, the wooden church from Mirosloveşti, the wooden Lipovan Church from Brătești, Siret Valley, Natural Reservation Curţile Zmeilor, traditional Moldovan houses (Boureni, Homița, Verşeni, Mirosloveşti), Lipovaneşti (Brătești), Rroma (Moţca)

Total length: 92 km
Vertical climb: 1770 m
Maximum altitude: 451 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes up to 13%)
Road: DN, DJ, DC, agricultural and forestry roads
Degree of difficulty: Difficult;
Recommended bike type: eMTB / Mountain-bike;
Cyclist profile: Trained amateur/ Cyclists
Time duration: 1-2 days (depending on experience level);
