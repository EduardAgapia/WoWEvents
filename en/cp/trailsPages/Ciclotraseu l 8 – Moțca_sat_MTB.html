Cycloroute Moţca village

Description

It is a short cycling route dedicated also to families and children over 10 years old, which starts from the parking lot of Moțca City Hall and which aims to tour Moţca village and promote anthropogenic heritage objectives. The route is the easiest in terms of effort, measuring only 7 km and a level difference of 70 m, and most of it is traveled on asphalt communal roads. The main objectives of interest are visiting the museum village, village church in old style, old streets dotted with traditional houses with specific local architecture (including houses specific to the Roma community in Moţca). As the cycling route will cross DN28A several times which is highly transited to peak hours, for the safety of groups of children is mandatory and participation of an adult or local guide

Total length: 7 km
Vertical climb: 70 m
Maximum altitude: 341 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes up to 8%)
Road: DC (partly asphalt, gravel), DN, farm road and trail
Degree of difficulty: Easy;
Recommended bike type: Mountain bike / Trekking;
Cyclist profile: Amateur/ Families with children/ Children +10 years
Journey time: 1 1/2 - 2 hours;
