Moţca ATV Cycloroute

Description

This route aims to highlight to tourists the beauties of the surroundings of Codri Pașcanilor. Is an easy route for ATV, but also suitable for electric bicycles (eMTB) having a level difference of 290m. The starting point of the route is the headquarters of the Codru LAG Pașcanilor from Moţca, after which we turn right on a secondary asphalted road to He admired some peasant households on the old street of the village. The trail then climbs the valley of a streams, among cereal fields, after which we go out on the summit to admire the village of Cristesti from above. We head to the forest, then turn left on the paved road to reach Cabana Vânătorească Moţca, a favorite place for members of the surrounding local communities, where we do and a hydration break. From here, we return to the same road on the paved road, turn left until in DN28A and climb to the Soviet Cemetery and St. Theodora Monastery in Codrii Pașcanilor. From here We head to the edge of the forest and turn right onto the farm road in the direction of Boureni. We pass through wheat and corn fields, and climb until we meet the paved road, we turn again right, after which we carefully descend directly to the Boureni Hermitage. After another hydration break, we We go to the village hearth to admire the traditional houses and the village church, then we we continue the route to Moţca on the agricultural exploitation road through cereal fields. We climb about 1 km and turn left on the old cart road that once connected the villages of Boureni and Moţca. We make a last stop to admire the beautiful view over the Valley of Moldova and Stanisoara mountains, after which we carefully descend to Moţca village. Arriving in the village turn right on one of the streets of the village that leads us to Moţca City Hall. From here, we still have about 200m climbing the DN28A to the LAG headquarters, the end point of the route.

Total length: 19 km
Vertical climb: 290 m
Maximum altitude: 450 m
Terrain: hilly (with slopes up to 20%)
Road: DN, DC, agricultural and forestry road
Degree of difficulty: Medium;
Recommended bike type: ATV / eMTB;
Cyclist profile: amateur or professional ATV (requires driving permit)
Journey time: 3 - 3 1/2 h;
