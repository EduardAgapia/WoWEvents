Boureni Hermitage

Description

Established many centuries before, Boureni Hermitage, located in the village of Boureni, by the NINETEENth century it was disbanded and turned into a parish when they was secularized monastic fortunes. Later on, on at the end of the XIX century, it was re-founded by the Family of the Thrushesti, being put under the oblăduirea Neamţ monastery. After the reconstruction of the hermitage, at the beginning of the century al. XX-th, in the precinct was entered under the bell tower that had two levels, in the upper one being 2 bells, which they sounded very harmonious. It had a small wooden church, which in 1939 was consecrated by the bishop vicar Valeriu Moglan. In the church, there was a miracle-working icon of the Mother of God, like that of at Neamţ Monastery

Location

Moțca, Boureni village
