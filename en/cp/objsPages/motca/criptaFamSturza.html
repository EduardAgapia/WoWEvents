Sturza-Micle family crypt

Description

Dimitrie-Mihail Sturza, son, was residing in Boureni initially illegitimate of Prince Grigore M. Sturza, but subsequently adopted. This building no longer exists, but it does it was in the space where the General School is located today. The prince's consort, Valeria (Micle) Sturza was the daughter of the great one of Veronica Micle with Stefan Micle. After a career of mezzo-soprano, Valeria settles in the mansion at Boureni in 1903 where he marries with Dimitrie and where he raises 4 children. The Prince had wanted to Valeriei's build a palace near the old hermitage, but a financial crisis stopped his project. A clum of pine trees remained as proof where a mortuary chapel was built and Valeria (1929) with two of her sons were burried. After being desecrated in the 1960s, the earthly remains of the three were displaced, probably to Măn. Văratec, where the tomb of Veronica Micle is also located.

Location

Moțca, Boureni village
