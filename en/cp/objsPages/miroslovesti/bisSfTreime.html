Holy Trinity Church

Description

The church dedicated to the Holy Trinity in Miroslovești has was built in 1921, the interior painting dating back all of that Period. Currently, the church it is in the process of being renovated. On the exterior architecture of the church, we observe certain decorative elements such as the painting behind the church, representing the Saint Third. Surrounding the church we notice a row of carved seals on colored discs with different reasons. Part of these decorative elements were removed due to renovation.

Location

Miroslovești, Miroslovești village
