The wooden church dedicated to Pious Parascheva

Description

The wooden church dedicated to Pious Parascheva from the village of Mirosloveşti, the commune of Mirosloveşti is included in the list of historical monuments under the code LMI IS-II-m-B-04205. The church was built in the new part of the village, "in Năvrăpeşti, in the hill's bat", above the river Moldova, dedicated to St. Pious Paraschiva in the year 1756, which is recorded in an inscription above Entry. It shows the specific features of the style Moldovan, distinguished in the vaulted dome over the ship and lateral apses. It is also noted the three registers carved with much effect around the church's stump: a belt in rope doubled by a wave of water over the sole, a median girdle of embossed crosses and a row of obits under the eaves.

Location

Miroslovești, Miroslovești village
