"Saint Emperors Constantine and Helen" Church

Description

Church of the Saint Emperors Constantine and Helen in the village of Sochi it was built since 2013 at the initiative of the priest Romică Simiciuc. Also at his initiative, a gifted cross was erected with two cannons for the 500 Romanian soldiers fallen in the battles of Sochi-Boureni-Brăteşti (1944) and who were buried in the old cemetery, this monument being cared for by pupils and young people Parish.

Location

Miroslovești, Soci village
