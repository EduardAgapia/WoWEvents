Homița Forest

Description

The Homița Forest natural area is a site of community importance located on the administrative territory of the communes of Cristeşti and Moţca. Having the code ROSCI0159, the area is part of the Natura 2000 network, being introduced in 2007. With an area of 57 ha, the protected area has a value of biodiversity due to the natural habitats of vegetation of the Eurosiberian forest-steppe with oak. Same as the Tătăruși Forest reserve, a series of rare plants such as the lady's slipper (Cypripedium calceolu) and cosash (Isophya stysi) are found inside this area, strengthening the need to protect this territory.

Location

Cristești - Moțca
