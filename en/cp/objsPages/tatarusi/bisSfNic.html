Saint Nicholas Church

Description

The church dedicated to "St. Nicholas" from Tătăruși village it was built in 1842. It was founded by the villagers. The edifice is built in style Moldovan, the church having the shape of a cross, with a spire above the narthex. Materials used in Construction are stone and brick. Inside, the church holds a naïve mural, instead there are icons dating from the year of construction, wooden iconostasis (1929) and various objects of ritual, among which there are also books dating from the XVIII-XIX centuries, written in cyrillic alphabet. In 1890 it was closed for repairs, but following the death of the parish priest, it remained closed for the next 30 years. In 1929 it is restored, the walls being restored from the collapsed vaults.

Location

Tătăruși
