Pios Parascheva Church (Herești Parish)

Description

The church dedicated to Saint Pious Parascheva in Cristeşti village was built in 1976 on the place an old dilapidated church. The new building had to impress with its appearance as a cathedral, by its dimensions and positioning, guarding the entire settlement. The construction works were completed after 16 months with the help of locals of the whose concerns were largely focused on construction in that Period. In 2007, at The parish of Heresti were brought the vestments that have adorned the relics of Saint Paraskevi, but also a part of the relics of Saint Haralambie. Year by year year, here are organized services on the occasion of the feast of Saint Pious Paraskeva, in which hundreds participate of pilgrims.

Location

Cristești, Cristești village
