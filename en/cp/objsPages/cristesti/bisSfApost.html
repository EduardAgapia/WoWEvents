Church of the Holy Apostles

Description

The wooden church of the Holy Apostles from the village of Homița dates from 1853 and is included in the List historical monuments in Iaşi County, with code IS-II-m-B-04182 (historical monument of local interest). According to a document from the parish archive, seen by the priest Vasile Vatamanu, the parish would have been founded in around 1840. About the foundation of the village Homița is not known exact data, but the local tradition says that after the departure of the Lipovans, the believers of Orthodox religion built here a small church of wood, which was subsequently destroyed during the time of the world war II (1944). Residents have rebuilt church in 1950, keeping the architecture of the original church (cross shape, two apses under pentagonal shape).

Location

Cristești, Homita village
