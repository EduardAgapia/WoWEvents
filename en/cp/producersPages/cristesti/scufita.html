"Scufița" Farm in Homița

Description

The spouses Laura and Antim Nechifor, horticulturists by profession, after 7 years of living in the capital, retired in grandparents' village to grow vegetables in ecologically regenerative system. They produce 54 varieties of tomatoes from a rich variety of colors and shapes. In great demand are yellow and purple carrots, not missing the classic orange carrots, Asian leaves for salads, leaf mustard, mangold, tomatillo, etc.
Contact: scufitadinhomita@gmail.com

Location

Cristești, Homița village
