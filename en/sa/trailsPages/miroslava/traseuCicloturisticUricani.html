Uricani Cycling Trail

Description

In Miroslava was inaugurated in 2015 the cycling route "Green Tunnel Uricani – Sturdza Miroslava Palace". The route stretches over 13 km of paved road, road forest and trails in the forest. The cycling route highlights the nature and monuments of the commune: Sturdza Palace Miroslava – Uricani Green Tunnel. The beauty of this route is especially given by the covering the approximately 6 km, through the Uricani Forest, through a labyrinth of paths. The degree of difficulty of this route is an easy one and is recommended for cycling in the family, especially in the forest portion.
