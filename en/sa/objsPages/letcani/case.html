Traditional houses

Description

Visitors to the village can also see traditional houses in Lețcani. The oldest is from 1957, the year being crowned by carpenters in horboţică. The houses that survived the modernization of the village have color blue and are covered with tin. They consist of three parts: tend , pantry and big house. The houses from Lețcani, like some of the fountains, are the fruit of imagination and talent peasant. They must be preserved and valued (village museum).

Location

Lețcani
