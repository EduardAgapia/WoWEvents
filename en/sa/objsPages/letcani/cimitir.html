Heroes cemetery

Description

In Lețcani there is one of the largest cemeteries of heroes in Romania. Here they are buried 1114 Romanian soldiers who fell in August 1944 when the front line was right here. Also in the cemetery there are 30 German soldiers and a Russian. After the Second World War the crosses from the graves were removed and the cemetery was turned into pasture. Only in the year 1992 the crosses were relocated after the notes of a local, Ionita, who had noted sites before being removed during the communist period. It is worth mentioning that the Association of War Veterans also placed in the 90's the bust of Marshal Ion Antonescu. He was later removed because Antonescu was considered a war criminal and The Romanian legislation regulates such cases. It remained only the socket on which they were placed two flags.

Location

Lețcani
