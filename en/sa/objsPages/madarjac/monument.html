Monument to the heroes

Description

In the center of Mădârjac commune there is a monument dedicated to the heroes of the War of Independence in 1877, to those fallen in the First and Second World War. The monument is represented by a woman with a flower in her right hand that signifies Freedom. On the side, the right, is a bronze fresco. The monument was made by the well-known plastic artist Iftimie Bârleanu. Without a doubt it is a unique monument worthwhile valued. The bronze woman made by Bârleanuș and which we discover today in Mădârjac was presented in 1980 at a Exhibition in Moscow receiving the gold medal. The commune but also the Silver Oaks LAG have the chance extraordinary to have on the territory a monument that deserves to be known and highlighted. Many cities in Romania would certainly like a work of the great artist Iftimie Bârleanu.

Location

Mădârjac
