The Church of the Nativity of the Mother of God

Description

The Church of the "Nativity of the Mother of God" of Miroslava is on the list of historical monuments in Ia≈üi County. It was built in 1811 but is mentioned in a document in 1833. On the south side of the church there is a marble monument made by Olga Sturdza. Under the monument is deposited the remains of Prince Mavrocordatos who died in 1907 in Paris. The patron saint of the church is celebrated every year on September 8.

Location

Miroslava, Miroslava village
