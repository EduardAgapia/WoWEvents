Bivolari Center for Medico-Social Assistance

Description

Bivolari commune has a center of medical-social assistance, which provides assistance for 60 elderly people with diseases chronic, after the center of Sculeni was Disbanded. In the medical dispensary activates 3 family doctors, 3 health professionals and 1 doctor dentist. Talking about the history of construction of a hospital in the commune, in 1843, prince Michael Sturdza issued a charter for the establishment of the fair Bivolari, on the property of hetman Iordache Ghica, and in 1844 an agreement was concluded between owner and bargains, in which one of the obligations it was the granting of land for the construction of a hospital. This was so postponed that between the years 1881 - 1882, on the land received as a donation from the peasant Ion Luca Ciobanu, the county authorities decided the construction of a hospital (today's center), which was, moreover, the first rural hospital in Iasi County, with a capacity of 12 beds. Unfortunately, the hospital burned on February 13, 1912, the hospital being rebuilt between 1913 - 1914. The building now had 20 rooms and a capacity of 30 beds. Between 1973 - 1975 a a wing was built upstairs, and the capacity increased to 121 beds.
