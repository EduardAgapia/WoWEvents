Prut river

Description

Sci-type protected natural area (site of importance) Community) , the Prut River has an area of 11861 ha protected, it stretches over the territory of Ia≈üi and Vaslui counties. This protected area was established in 2007, being an integral part of the network nature 2000 with the code ROSCI0213. The protected area is located within the minor riverbed and the major riverbed of the Prut River. The presence of many habitats present in the site (mixed riparian forests, dystrophic lakes and eutrophes or natural meadows of low altitudes) of allowed the development of a rich diversity. Remarkable for this area is the ichthyofauna, this being considered very rich.
