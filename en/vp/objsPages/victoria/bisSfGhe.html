"Saint George" village

Description

St. George's Church in frăsuleni village was built somewhere between 1855-1862 by Petru Voinescu and repaired in 1971 by endeavor of Andrei Iordache (according to some documents). In the same year, the church was re-consecrated. Between 1903 - In 1905, the church was consolidated. This is built of brick, shaped like a ship and has a single spire. In 2003, it started renovation of the church. The walls were plastered to be repainted and the iconostasis was replaced by a new linden wood one, made entirely by the epitrope of the parishRăileanu Mircea.

Location

Victoria, Frăsuleni village
