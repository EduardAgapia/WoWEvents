The Rock Massacre

Description

In June 1941, at the Roznovanu Rock took place one of the massacres during the Pogrom, committed by 6th Hunters Regiment. About the massacres from Sculeni and Gura Căinari there are numerous documents in the archives, however, the testimonies of the people helped to reconstruct the atmosphere. Following the researches archaeological carried out in the years 2010-2013 were discovered the mass graves where they were gathered Jews. On the site of the rock massacre, in the islaz near the village, a monument was erected commemorative in 2000 by an American Jewish organization.

Location

Victoria, Stânca village
