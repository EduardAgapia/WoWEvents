Saint Nicholas Church

Description

The church "St. Nicholas" from luceni village was built in the early nineteenth century, on the place of the older one, made of wood, which as it's being circulated, it's been burned to the ground. She is founded by the boyar Nicolae Rosetti (Ruset) Roznovanu. The masonry works were completed in the year 1808, and its consecration was made in the year 1810. Thus, with the passage of time this the church became the oldest remaining edifice, being on this occasion reckoned as a monument of church architecture with a real load Historic. The walls of 1.20 meters stand as testimony to the resistance in time of the church. However, with the passage of time, the various problems related to the way have also arisen. especially the quality of the exterior and interior finishing of the wall. The place of worship was included in the List Historical Monuments, code IS-II-m-B-04192.

Location

Victoria, Luceni village
