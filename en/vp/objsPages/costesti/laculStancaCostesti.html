Stânca Costești Lake Reservation

Description

The area "Lacul Stânca – Costeşti" (2.950 ha) was declared a natural reservation by the Decision of Government No. 2151 of November 30, 2004 (establishing the protected area regime for us zones). A significant area of it (2,161 ha) entered the Natura 2000 network as sci-type reserve - special avifaunistic protection area. The territory is classified in the bioregion continental of the Moldavian platform in the upper basin of the Prut river and represents the artificial lake created through a dam on the Prut River (upstream of the eponymous hydropower plant), in the north-eastern part of Romania (at the border with the Republic of Moldova); and the areas adjacent to it (ponds, reedbeds, păpuriș, wooded areas, meadows). Often here come fishermen and archaeologists (in the Ripiceni area). The site has three classes of habitats natural type: Continental freshwater (stagnant, flowing); Extensive cereal crops (including rotational crops with de-icing) and improved grasslands; which shelter and ensure favorable conditions of nesting and feeding of several species of birds (migratory, overpassing or sedentary), of which there are some protected at European level or on the IUCN Red List.

Location

Costești
