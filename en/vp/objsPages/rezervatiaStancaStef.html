"Stânca Ștefănești" reservation

Description

Stanca Ştefănești is a protected territory of the type a reserve of a complex, geological character, and floristic, located northeast of the town of Ştefăneşti, Botoşani County. The rock appears on the surface as a calcareous "spur" formed by deposits of luturi quaternary loessoids, with a maximum height of 101m, known as the "House of the Lady". This karst reef shelters the coasts rocky a very rare plant- Schivereokia podolic. For this species, here is the limit the west of its world area. Floristic species Schivereckia podolica is critically threatened because the habitat of the species was largely destroyed by the construction of the Stanca-Costeşti dam.

Location

to the North-East of the town of Ştefăneşti, Botoşani County
