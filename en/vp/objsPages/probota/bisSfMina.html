„St. Mina” Church

Description

The church in probota village has two dedications - "St. Mina" and "St. Basil the Great" and was built in 1893, from the contributions of the villagers. The place of worship is 18 m long and 6 m wide, and the painting on the iconostasis has an artistic value special, but the name of the artist is unknown. In the churchyard there is a cemetery of heroes Soviets of the Second World War.

Location

Probota, Perieni village
