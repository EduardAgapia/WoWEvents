Secondary School „GH.CIOBANU”

Description

Between 1908 and 1920 the school courses were held in the house of the teacher Gheorghe Ciobanu. This a teacher was designated as the symbol of the school as he was a principal between 1890 and 1930, as well as deputy in the Parliament of Romania between 1919 and 1923. On the current site, the school was established in year 1920 and functioned in the first building with two small halls that are now halls of kindergarten. Between 1939 and 1943, the second building (B) was erected. It was inaugurated on September 6th 1943 and at that time the new building was appreciated "as the only place in the region that deserves all admiration". Between 1967 and 1969, the A building of the school where secondary school students study was erected.

Location

Andrieșeni, Andrieșeni village
