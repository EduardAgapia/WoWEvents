The old Church of St. Arch. Michael and Gabriel

Description

The old church of St. Arch. Michael and Gabriel from the village Bădărăi is in the same courtyard as the new Church. The wooden church was built in 1896 from the orders of Mihail Bădărău, who came from Bessarabia to build a place of worship in Moldova. Inside the church is the Holy Table which it is built on oak trunk. Since 2014, the parish priest is Mihai Bodea who does his best to raise funds to renovate the wooden church. Currently not it is still served at all in it, but it can be admired on the outside.

Location

Santa Mare, Bădărăi village
