"St. George" Church

Description

In the New Dămideni Village began the construction of a new church place in 1989, through the endeavor priest Vasile Marineac. After the realization of the foundations and a part of masonry, the works have terminated due to lack of funds. Human intervention of business Gelu Tofan, married mariana (Onofrei) from Dămideni, determined the completion its construction and consecration in 1999. Assembly includes the "Saint George" Church and the tower of at the entrance to the courtyard.

Location

Românești, Dămideni village
