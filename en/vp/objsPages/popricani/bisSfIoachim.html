Church of St. Joachim and Anna

Description

The church dedicated to "The Holy Fathers Joachim and Anna" it is located in the center of the village Cuza Voda, its construction starting in 1954 and continuing until 1959, when the Iași Popular Council stops the works, thanks to the policies of that time. The works were resumed only in 1970, being completed two years later, when it is sanctified by His Eminence Metropolitan Justin. The church is built in traditional Moldavian style, having a shape clubs. Near the place of worship there is a social house, which serves as a room for memorials, but also a library with over 600 books. Between 2008 - 2011, the new parish house was built.

Location

Popricani, Cuza Vodă village
