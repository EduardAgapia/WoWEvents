Memorial monuments / traces of war

Description

The inhabitants of Popricani commune participated in events that they marked the history of the twentieth century, such as the War of Independence and the two world wars. In the time of the of the Second World War, the men of Popricani fought on eastern front, at the Don Bend (where the most tragic took place military operation in the history of the Romanian army, with approximately 150000 dead), in Odessa and Stalingrad. One of the inhabitants, Toader Roșcan, was a child during the war, but he told the story how his father, Neculai Roșcan (born in 1912) fought for country: "Dad was incorporated from the beginning of the war, fought and in Popricani, and in Moimeşti, and at the Roznovanu Castle in The rock."
- Iron cross for heroes - 1985 - in the courtyard of st. Nicholas Popricani church
- Carved oak cross - 2005 - the center of popricani village

Location

Popricani
