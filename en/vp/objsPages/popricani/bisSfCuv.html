Church of St. Pious Parascheva

Description

The Church of Moimeşti, dedicated to "Saint Pious" Parascheva", was built in 2003, even if the place for the erection of the church was donated much earlier, in 1924. The works were completed only in 2012. Until 1920, on the place of this church there was an old church of wood, founded by Ana Tăutu in 1840, which wore the patron saint of "St. Basil". From this church were kept the iconostasis and iconostasis, moved to a church in the village of Ciulineşti (photo), and in the courtyard is finds a cross erected on the site of the former church. In the year In 2000, a feast house was erected. Only in in 2002, moimești branch becomes a parish. The church was consecrated on July 7, 2019 by his Eminence Father Teofan, Metropolitan of Moldavia and Bukovina, together with a group of priests and Deacons.

Location

Popricani, Moimești village
