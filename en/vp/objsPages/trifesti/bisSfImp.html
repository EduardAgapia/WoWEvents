Church of the Holy Emperors Constantine and Helen

Description

The church is dedicated to "The Holy Emperors Constantine and Elena" was built in the year 1839. It is considered to be 'one of the historical monuments of the locality". This is the foundation of Costache Negruzzi's father, the backrest of Dinu Negruț. The architecture of the place of worship is in the classical style, and to the interior the mural painting is in style neobizantin. The church was rehabilitated in 1935. In the cemetery of the church are found the tombs of the family Negruzzi. LMI: IS-II-m-B-04193.01 (part of the Ensemble Negruzzi family yard)

Location

Trifești, Hermeziu village
