Costache Negruzzi memorial house

Description

The house where the writer Costache Negruzzi grew up is dated from 1807-1810. It belonged to in the first phase to the writer's parents, Dinu Negruțși Sofia Hermeziu, received from her family following the marriage of the two. In addition to those mentioned, other personalities of the family, including: Leon C. Negruzzi, Iacob Negruzzi, Mihai Negruzzi, Ella Negruzzi (First woman lawyer from Romania) and Leon M. Negruzzi. In the form of a museum-memorial house it was opened from October 7, 1995. Exhibition permanent includes five rooms that want to faithfully reproduce the life of the Negruzzi family in the centuries previous, and in the entrance hall there are various documents and manuscripts attesting the life of families boyar Negruzzi and Hermeziu

Location

Trifești, Hermeziu village
