"Saint Elijah" Church

Description

The church dedicated to "Saint Elijah" from Soloneț village it was built in 1820, with the founder of the homemaker Athanasius Gosan. This church is the only objective in the commune of Bivolari included in the List of Historical Monuments in Iaşi County, quoted IS-II-m-B-04248. Since 2006, the parish publishes monthly the "Word of Soloneț", newspaper that aims to inform parishioners, by cultural and educational articles. The newspaper has even a section of Legal Consultations, addressed persons over the age of 60. In 2009, the parish priest and the parish committee organized the third edition of the children's toast contest, entitled "Toaca-n cer" . The patron saint of the church, believers must take part in the agape organized in the churchyard, where they taste the dishes prepared by the village housewives.

Location

Bivolari, Soloneț village
