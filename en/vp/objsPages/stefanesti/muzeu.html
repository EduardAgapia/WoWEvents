„Ștefan Luchian” museum - house

Description

House - museum is the only one dedicated to the artist Stefan Luchian. It is open to the public from 2021 onwards in a building that originally shelter the school. Within this museum there are pieces of art plastic and sculpture belonging to the collection Botoşani County Museum. In the same building, the painter's "workshop-room" was reconstituted, using various items donated on behalf of the foundation dedicated to the artist. The house dedicated to the artist presents more his life, within which only one painting is present originally painted by Stefan Luchian, namely a "Landscape"

Location

Ștefănești
