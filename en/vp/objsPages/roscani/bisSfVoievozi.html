Church of The Holy Princes Michael and Gabriel

Description

The wooden church of St. Voivodes in the village of Rădeni has it was built in the XVII century (1780), on the site of an older church from 1485. The Church has it was completely rebuilt between 1818 and 1819, and subsequently, a few years later it was decided to plaster the walls so that you can no longer see the wooden beams. Above the porch is a bell tower. Since 2004, the place of worship has been included on the List historical monuments with the code IS-II-m-B-04233.

Location

Roșcani, Rădeni village
