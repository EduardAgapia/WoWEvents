Lungu Domains/ Hermeziu Bivolari Winery

Description

The bivolari farm was founded in 2001 and covers an area of 2000 Hectares. It supports activities in 5 main areas, namely: viticulture, horticulture, agriculture, animal husbandry, and tourism. Lungu domains use Advanced technologies in the methods of cultivation, harvesting, processing and storage, to obtain quality products. Group of companies Domeniile Lungu has in 130 permanent employees and 5 privately owned companies, and namely AGRO INDUSTRIAL CERES SRL, LORVAS COMPANY SRL, AGRICOLA ASTRA SRL, FLAVISEM CORPORATION SRL, MITVAS TOUR SRL, SOCIETATEA AGRICOLĂ ASTRA TRIFEŞTI.
