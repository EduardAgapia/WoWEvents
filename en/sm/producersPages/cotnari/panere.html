Panere - Cerasus Grup

Description

Panere is the registered trademark of Cerasus Grup, a producer organization with 105 ha of cherry orchards, apples, pears and plums located in Cotnari, Iasi, Romania. It arose in 2006 from the need of manufacturers to fruits from the area to associate, because together it's always easier. Immediately after its establishment, Cerasus Grup obtained from the Ministry of Agriculture the status of Producer Group, being the first such group from the area of Moldova and among the first in the country. The producer organization Cerasus Grup is in continuous development of production capacities, fruit conditioning and storage capacities, as well as sales channels.
