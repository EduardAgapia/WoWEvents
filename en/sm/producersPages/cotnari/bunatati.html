Homemade Goodies - Elena Cocuța Bujor

Description

The pandemic has hastened the implementation of the small children's initiative farmers to process their own vegetables and fruits, as well as and the association of small local producers, in order to capitalize on the common everything they get. Mrs. Elena Cocuța Bujor owns 2 ha of garden and 3 ha of orchard from which he got vegetables and fruits that he sold directly. In recent times years focused on processing crops (products canned from vegetables and fruits, zacusca, salads, etc.) after recipes from childhood. He also opened his own store at Cotnari, called "Goodies from home". In addition to the 5 hectares with vegetables and fruit trees, Elena Cocuţa Bujor it also raises 3,000 chickens. The range of activities was diversified and through an investment in a bakery-pastry workshop. From the desire to get food as healthy as possible, iasi has started the process of certification in organic system of vegetable production, with completion in 2022. In addition, it has taken the first steps to certify as a traditional product the pies Moldovan "poale-n brâu".

Location

Cotnari
