Cotnari Wine House

Description

Cotnari Wine House is an ambitious project, started in 2007, with the establishment in the vineyard of new plantations, consisting of the traditional Grasa varieties by Cotnari, Tămâioasă Românească and Fetească Albă, who were joined by Busuioaca and Feteasca Neagră. Cotnari Wine House was born from the initiative of the second generation of the main shareholders of at SC COTNARI SA, the largest producer in cotnari vineyard, which, with the passage of years, has passed its manifested the desire to complement the assortments of wine obtained in the vineyard with premium white, rose wines and tomatoes, dry or semi-dry, intended exclusively for the HoReCa segment and specialized stores. For these reasons, the Cotnari Wine House appeared, a distinct company, based in the Castle Vlădoianu, which owns land and vineyards planted in 2007, Vlădoianu Winery – a modern winery, completed and Axinte Winery – a recently acquired winery to be upgraded.

Location

Cotnari, Cârjoaia village
