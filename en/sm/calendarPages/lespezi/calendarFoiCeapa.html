Calendar of onion sheets

Description

The calendar of onion sheets is made around the time of winter customs. Take an onion, unfold 12 sheets (each representing one month of the year), place in a row and put a little salt. In which sheet there is more water, that month will be rainier. The calendar is true if the one who has it made is a faithful man and kept all the fasting of Christmas.

Location

Lespezi
