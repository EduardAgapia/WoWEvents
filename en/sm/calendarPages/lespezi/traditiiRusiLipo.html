Traditions and customs of lipovan Russians in the commune of Lespezi

Description

Currently, there are around 50 Lipovan Russian families in Lespezi. They settled in two mini-neighborhoods, Median and mahala, they built a church in Mahala, where they went to pray on Sundays and on days of feast, dressed in traditional clothes made by them. Examples of Lipovan habits:
- Masleni»õa (Cheese Week) - people celebrate with cheerful songs and dairy dishes specific (varenichi, blini, pyroghi)
- The flower - the floor of the church is covered with a carpet of grass, and the faithful hold candles in their hands and holy willow twigs
- The wedding lasts three days and specific songs are hummed, music being forbidden

Location

Lespezi
