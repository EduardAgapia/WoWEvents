The Royal Cellars in the Palace Princely

Description

LMI code: IS-II-m-A-04135.02
It is believed that a princely court was established in Cotnari during his reign Stephen the Great. From here several royal acts were issued as early as 1454. The cellar kept here could have been built for the storage of the Reign's winery, by some princely governor or by any monastery who had more extensive properties at Cotnari.

Location

Cotnari, Cotnari village
