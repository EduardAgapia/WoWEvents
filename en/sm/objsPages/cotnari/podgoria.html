Cotnari Vineyard

Description

The Cotnari vineyard bears the history of over 2000 years of a legendary land and the unbroken tradition of production of an authentic Romanian wine, synonymous with the excellence of the craft of winemaking. That's how it is that the first international distinction was obtained in the times of the ruler Al. I. Cuza. Cotnari vineyard is especially famous for the traditional white varieties: Grasă de Cotnari, Tămâioasă Romanian and Fetească Albă, who were joined by Busuioaca (vinified rose) and Feteasca Neagră, for red varieties. Plantations at Cotnari pursue a low density of plants, a large distance between rows, which allows an efficient air circulation and carrying out work with modern machinery. Cotnari Wine it needs a ripening in peace, in oak buds, for up to 2 years. Then it is bottled or kept in the wine cellar to enjoy with its bouquet future generations. There is no time in the life of a man when Cotnari wine is not by his side. Successfully accompanies dishes from the finest, from the main dishes, to dessert. It is a gift of the soul, surprising and always in good taste. Cotnari wine is not drunk. He tastes himself. He is divine promise. When accompanies a lasting feast, drinking sparingly, develops noble, amazing reactions.
