Church of St. Arch. Michael and Gabriel

Description

The Church of St. Archangels Michael and Gabriel was built during the reign of Stephen the Great of to a captain named Stroie. The church has passed through different rehabilitation processes, the last being between years 1998-2000. Subsequently, between 2000-2001 it was achieved iconostasis, after the painted process began inside the church until 2007. The church has the appearance of a fortress, with stone walls of almost two meters high. The church is made up of three canonical rooms: altar, nave, narthex. A Original element from the time of Stephen the Great is the entrance door of the church. It is in the List of Historical Monuments with the code IS-II-a-A-04251.

Location

Todirești, Stroești village
