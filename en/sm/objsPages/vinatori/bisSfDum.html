St. Demetrius Church

Description

The church dedicated to "Saint Demetrius" from the village Criveşti dates from the NINETEENth century, being built in the shape of a cross, in the Moldavian decadent style. The church has a small wooden porch at the entrance. The altar has a semicircular shape, with two niches Side. Inside there are donated feast icons by the Gheorghiade family, and the iconostasis is constructed of wood and ornamented with carvings in vegetable motifs, such as grapes or leaves of vineyard. Over time, the church was renovated by several times, without changing its appearance originally architectural.

Location

Vânători, Crivești village
