Church of St. Arch. Michael and Gabriel

Description

The church dedicated to "The Holy Archangels Michael and Gavriil" was built between 1966 - 1978, being placed at the entrance to the village cemetery. Changes political in the 50s made the process of lifting difficult of the church, so that in 1966, the parish councilors decide the erection of a mortuary chapel near the cemetery in Poieniţa, but the builders modified the project without approval, resulting in the present church. Painting the interior was made in oil, and the iconostasis it is carved in wood. With the help of parishioners and parish priest, in recent years have been achieved numerous restoration, construction and the modernization of the church, the cemetery and the feast house, even a parish library being established. In the churchyard is a monument to the martyr heroes of the two world wars.

Location

Vânători, Hârtoape village
