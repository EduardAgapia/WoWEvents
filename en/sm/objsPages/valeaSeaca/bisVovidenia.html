Vovidenia Church

Description

Vovidenia Church, dedicated to "The Entrance of the Mother The Lord in the Church", from valea seacă village was originally built of wood, by Voivode Duca and his wife, Anastasia (XVII century). Due to damages suffered in time, roznovanu boyars they built the current stone church in the second half of the XIX century. Initially it was both monastic hermitage, as well as a parish church. Outside it has been completely restored.

Location

Valea Seacă, Valea Seacă village
