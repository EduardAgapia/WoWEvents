Church of St. Arch. Michael and Gabriel

Description

Church historical monument (LMI: IS-II-m-B-04273) dedicated to "St. Arch. Michael and Gabriel" was built at 1666. The year of construction was estimated following a dendrological analysis of the oak beams used in structure. It is believed that Enache the Elder would be the founder of the church. It is the only wooden church - historical monument from Iasi county in that is still being served. The plan of the church is an ordinary one, with narthex, nave and altar, but have an architectural peculiarity. Between narthex and nave there is a wooden parting that resembles a iconostasis. It has existed since the time of construction and is not specific to the area. The iconostasis is not the original one, but was brought from an old hermitage that was located in cotnari vineyards. In the Second World War, soldiers in the Soviet army turned it into a stable and occasionally they fired bullets into the cross and into icons to test their target. Still the holes in the bullets are observed.
