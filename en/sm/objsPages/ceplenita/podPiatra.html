Stone bridge at Zlodica

Description

RAN code: 96183.02
LMI code: IS-II-m-B-04274
Location: at the intersection of cotnari - Ceplenița road
Dating: Medieval Era (XV century)
The bridge is built in the XV century at the behest of Stephen the Great. The construction is 25-30 meters long, the width of about 6 meters and was made of carved river stone. The bridge connected the town of Harlau and Cotnari and is still used, including for cars. In time, some stones were removed by the villagers. In July 2022, the Ambulance for Monuments, through the Actum Association will travel to Zlodica for a intervention aimed at restoring the mortars that connect the masonry, as well as its additions. Because of repeated cycles of frost-thaw, the mortars are currently in an advanced state of decay, and some stones have dislocated.

Location

Ceplenița, Zlodica village
