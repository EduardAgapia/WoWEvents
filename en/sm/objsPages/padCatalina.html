Description

Cﾄフﾄネina Forest is a protected area since 2000, being classified in category IV IUCN - Nature Reserve of forest type). It is located at the contact area between the Plateau Suceava and the Moldavian Plain and has an area of 7.6 ha. Area protected, located on the administrative territory of cotnari commune it has a double meaning. At the highest point of the area, it is it also finds the Dacian Fortress from Cotnari. Also the reason the presence of the site is given by the presence of climatic factors special ones that create the premises for the development of a climate similar to that specific to the sub-Mediterranean area. This has led to the emergence of arboreal species of beech and sessile oak with ages between 150 and 200 years but also the presence of exotic species such as crimean beech (Fagus Taurica). Located in the immediate vicinity of cotnari village, but also of the Dacian fortress from Cotnari, the whole territory is shrouded in legends and mystery. However, the lack of knowledge or appreciation of the heritage (both the natural one as well as the archaeological one) can lead to the loss of these important elements that have the potential to attract Tourists.
