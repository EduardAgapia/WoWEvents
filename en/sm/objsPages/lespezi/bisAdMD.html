”Assumption of Mary” Church

Description

Assumption Church in the village Lespezi was built in 1854, on a plot of land donated by the boyar Bosie. The place of worship remains unfinished until the year 1869 when a handful of believers from Brăteni took the initiative to continue the church. It has been completed and consecrated in 1872. In the years 1957 and between 1975-1976 general repairs were carried out on the church. In year 1983 the icons are restored by prof. Grigorescu Mihail.

Location

Lespezi, Lespezi village
