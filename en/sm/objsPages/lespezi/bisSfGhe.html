"Saint George Church

Description

The church from Buda village is dedicated to St. George. The ecumenical objective was built on the site of a churches built of poplar wood and alder arrived in an advanced state of decay. The construction of the current church began in 1946, and the year 1955 was consecrated and received the patron "Saint George".
The architectural plan of the place of worship respects the style Moldovan churches, shaped like a ship with a tower at entrance, above the porch. The iconostasis was built of linden wood and painted in Byzantine style. In 1975 a bell tower was built, and the church was surrounded with a stone wall.
