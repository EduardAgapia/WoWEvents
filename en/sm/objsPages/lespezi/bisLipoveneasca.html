Lipovan Church

Description

The Old Rite Lipovan Church in the village of Lespezi is dedicated to the December 4, when it is celebrated ''The entrance of the Mother of God in Church'' and its dating is uncertain. According to the monograph Lespezi commune we find out that in 1880 there were 47 families of lipovans and the church was probably built in the same Period. It was destroyed by wars but was rebuilt and sanctified in 1948. Vovidenia Church has stone walls and is formed from the porch, nave, narthex and altar.

Location

Lespezi, Lespezi village
