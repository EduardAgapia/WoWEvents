"Saint Demetrius" Church

Description

The current Church of St. Demetrius in the village of Heci was built between 1920-1937, on the site of a former wooden churches, 216 years old. Belfry the place of worship was built in 5 years and the iconostasis is beautifully adorned and painted in the ''golden leaf'' technique. Between 1965-2005 the place of worship passed through many changes, the most representative is renovation in 2005 when the interior painting of the was made by the artist Ciprian Cârcei, from Grumăzeşti Neamţ. St. Demetrius Church in Heci has a capacity of 200 people.

Location

Lespezi, Heci village
