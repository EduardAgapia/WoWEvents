Church of St. Pious Parascheva

Description

The parish church from Badger Deal village is dedicated to "St. Pious Paraskeva" was built of wood in 1916 and consecrated in 1919. The current church, built with stone wall and brick, was erected between 1965-1967, painted and re-consecrated in 1969. The Church is going through a Period of Restoration made under the coordination of the parish priest Ion Luca: it was the painting of the church was restored, the iconostasis was replaced with one carved in cherry wood and painted using modern techniques.

Location

Lespezi, Bursuc Deal village
