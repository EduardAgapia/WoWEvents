The accumulations from Belcești - SPA

Description

The Site Of Accumulations Belcești, with the code ROSPA0109, was introduced in the Natura 2000 network in 2011. It has an area of 2099 ha, overlapping on the territory of the communes of Cotnari and Ceplenița in Lag Siret-Moldova. The site includes reservoirs, pastures and arable land, such as and clumps of forest and scrubs (located near wetlands). The territory has a diversity of remarkable pedo-climatic and hydrographic conditions, which is why a varied vegetation has been installed, with rich floristic elements and of different origins, the reason being the positioning in the contact area of the province Central European biogeographical with the Ponto-Sarmatic province. The territory of the site is an important area for aquatic and semi-aquatic bird species, especially during the migration period as it is the place of feeding and resting. According to the form of framing in the Natura 2000 network, there are over 36 species of birds, among which we mention the fallow tree (Platalea lucordia) as being declared a monument of nature. Another valuable species that is found on the territory of this site is the red-necked goose (Branta ruficollis), the species declared vulnerable with less than 50,000 copies worldwide.

Location

Cotnari and Ceplenița communes
