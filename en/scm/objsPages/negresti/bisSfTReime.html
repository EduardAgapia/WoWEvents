Holy Trinity Church

Description

In ancient times (XVI century) a hermitage dedicated to the Assumption of the Virgin Mary at the outskirts of Parpanița village during his reign Petru Rareş. The parish church was built between 1973- 1977, following the demolition of the old church, the construction of the being on another site(which was donated by a few families). The church is built in the form of ship, with a single tower, above the porch. Brick was used to lift it and the stone from the demolition of the old church, but both the iconostasis and the icons of the old church were preserved, this new church being built by the size of the old church. The church was not and is not painted. From the old church is preserved several church books from 1778, an epitaph (1780), a pomelnic (1819) and an octoih written in characters Cyrillic.

Location

Negrești, Parpanița village
