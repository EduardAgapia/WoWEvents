Rosetti-Balș Mansion

Description

Rosetti-Balş Mansion, in pribeşti village, Codăeşti commune, Vaslui county, had over time various owners, from the rosetti families (1691-1841) and Balș (1841-1962). After the death of Iordache Ruset, he left word to divide among his sons all properties in Ciorteşti, Pribeşti, Soleşti, Bălăneşti, Roznovăneşti. About the Balș family we can remember that two of the family members were masters of the mansion: Lupu Balşşi Eliza Balş. The story of the mansion in the possession of this family is quite hectic. Shortly after after buying the property, the governor Lupu Balș dies (1844), the heiress being his daughter, Eliza Balș. Eliza Balș was a personality who marked the history of Moldova through her marriages to Alexandru Moruzzi and Dumitru Beldiman. She had 4 children, but only one survived. Eliza, having a bad relationship with her daughter, Smaranda, makes the decision that in 1897 she will sell from properties, including the estate from Pribeşti.

Location

Codaesti, Pribești village
