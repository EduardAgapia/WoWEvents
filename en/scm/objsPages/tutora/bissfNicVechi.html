The old Church of Saint Nicholas

Description

The old church in the village of Chiperești was built around 1840-1845. The place of The construction chosen at that time was on a hill from the outskirts of the village, in the middle of the cemetery. The initial construction was made of wood and veils, with a shingle roof. Subsequently, in in 1917, the roof was restored from sheet metal black and a steeple was erected. In 1966 the roof was replaced again. The church has shape of vessel. On the inside does not present painting mural, and the icons were moved to the church nine. Over time they were made more many renovations and repairs. Since 1972, an active landslide gradually degrades the old church, forcing in time the village to resort to the construction of a new place of worship.

Location

Tutora, Chiperești village
