Dobrovăț Monastery

Description

Dobrovat monastery church began to build in 1503 and finished in 1504, being the last foundation of Stephen the Great. The royal inscription, written in Slavonic, on a slab of stone, and placed to the right of the door entrance, confesses: "Well-honored Lord Io Stephen the voivode, with the mercy of God the lord the country of Moldavia, the son of Bogdan Voevod, raised this feast day with the name of the Descent of the Holy Spirit, which began to be built in the year 7011 (1503), the month of April, in 27 days, and was committed in the year 7012 (1504) of his reign, the year forty-seven and one month of the eighth flowing."

Location

Dobrovăț
