”Holy Emperors” Church

Description

The parish church dedicated to "The Holy Emperors exactly with the Apostles, Constantine and Elena" from Comarna village was built in 1910. Regarding the architectural plan of the church, we note the crocheted shape (cross-shaped), the exterior being made of stone, with representations of saints in the upper area. The interior is painted in oil with certain carved elements on the iconostasis made by the former "School of Arts and Crafts" in Iasi.

Location

Comarna, Comarna village
