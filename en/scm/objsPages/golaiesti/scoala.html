Medeleni School/ Medeleni Mansion

Description

"Dear mother, I've gotten to Medeleni. What an admirable house Moldovan! Exactly the atmosphere of the we, amplified only as a reason musically played by the violin, then resumed by all the tools. I have the impression that relive the adorable pages of «The Life of in the countryside» by Duiliu Zamfirescu. It's all clean: the people, and the things, the sky and Landscape. At the end of the holiday I become poet! Don't worry!" - represents the words used by Ionel Teodoreanu to describes the manor from Medeleni, in the one of- the second volume of the novel of the same name. The mansion no longer exists, but the school building has been preserved. The action of the novel takes place in the school of Medeleni, this building is still standing today, and next to it is a small part of the park with exotic species that once existed. Can be observed species of pomiferous maclura - or in the popular name - false orange.

Location

Golăiești, Medeleni village
