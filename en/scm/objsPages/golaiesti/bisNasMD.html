Church of the Nativity of the Mother of God

Description

The church dedicated to the Nativity of the Mother of God in the village of Petreşti it was built in 1947, with the contribution of believers, being the third church built in this village - the first was overtaken in the Prut River in 1880, and the second that was inside the cemetery, it was destroyed in 1944, in wartime. The present church was built in the center of the village and has the shape of ship, with walls of earth and plank. In inside, the iconostasis is made of fir wood and oak, and the icons were painted in oil (those old Renaissance style, and new ones from the side from above, in byzantine style).

Location

Golăiești, Petrești village
