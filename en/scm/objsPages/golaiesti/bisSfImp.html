"Saint Emperors Constantine and Helen" Church

Description

The church dedicated to "The Holy Emperors Constantine and Helen" and "The Birth of Saint John The Baptist" was built after the revolution since 1989, through the efforts of the priest Ioan Roată from Medeleni. The new construction in the village is made of brick, has the shape of a cross with three Semicircular apses and a single spire on the nave. Inside, the iconostasis is made of wood carved, having three rows of icons in the side from above. The church is slender and simple and not shows interior or exterior painting. In the churchyard is located a very false orange old, planted by a boyar.

Location

Golăiești, Medeleni village
