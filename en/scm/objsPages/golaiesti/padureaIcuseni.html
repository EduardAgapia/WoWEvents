Icușeni (Golăiești) Forest

Description

The Icușeni Forest is a protected natural area of sci type, located on the administrative territory of the golaiesti commune, with an area of 11 hectares. It has been declared a protected natural area in 2000 and then a Natura 2000 site in 2007, with the code ROSCI0160. This forest represents a habitat area with Eurosiberian forest-steppe vegetation, with a protective role for secular species of trees such as oak and sessile oak. located in the immediate vicinity of the villages of Golăieşti and Cilibiu, the forest, located on the first terrace of the Jijia River, watches over the entire jijia valley and creates a cool microclimate during hot summers.

Location

Golăiești
