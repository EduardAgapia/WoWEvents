Saint Nicholas Church

Description

St. Nicholas Church in the village of Cilibiu is a historical monument (LMI code: IS-II-m-B-04121), dating from the XVIII century. Along of time, the original church has suffered damage and it was rebuilt between 1800 and 1850, by the family Sturdza, who built a new church on the site the old one, dedicated to "The Holy Archangels Michael and Gabriel". The current church, built of stone and brick, is the foundation of the great logofăt Iordache Ruset - Roznovanu. It has a plan triconc, has no spire, and was later added a porch. The elements of uniqueness are the dome imposing on the nave and the bricks laid "in serrated".

Location

Golăiești, Cilibiu village
