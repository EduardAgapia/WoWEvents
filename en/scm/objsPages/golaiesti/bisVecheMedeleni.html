The Old Church in Medeleni

Description

Old writings attest to the fact that on the right bank of the Prut there was a small church, built in 1845 by the boyar Alecu Donici, owner of the Medeleni Estate. The church was repaired in 1923, the founders being Gheorghe and Neculai Toma. The church was destroyed in 1944 and rebuilt in 1947, dedicated to "Pogorârea" Holy Spirit." The church has a clubs plan, a square-shaped porch and semi-circular altar, it is devoid of sculptural elements, or painting of special value and has no bell tower, the bell being installed in the porch.

Location

Golăiești, Medeleni village
