St. Parascheva Church

Description

The church dedicated to Saint Parascheva and The Holy Emperors Constantine and Helen from the village Golăieşti was built in 1812, from beams and rowdy, having a ship plan and a porch closed on which raises the bell tower. The walls of the church they do not have architectural ornamentation. The church was reconsolidated in 2000, a porch and a new bell tower.

Location

Golăiești, Golăiești village
