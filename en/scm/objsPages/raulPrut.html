Prut River

Description

The SCI -type protected natural area (site of community importance), Prut River has an area of 11861 of protected ha, this stretching on the territory of Iasi and Vaslui counties. This protected area was established in 2007, being an integral part of the Natura 2000 network with the code ROSCI0213. Protected area it is located within the minor riverbed and the major riverbed of the Prut River. The presence of numerous habitats present on the site (mixed riparian forests), dystrophic lakes and eutrophics or meadows natural low altitudes) allowed the development of a rich diversity. Remarkable for this area is ichthyofauna, which is considered very Rich.
