The Church of the Holy Voivodes

Description

The Church of the Holy Voivodes from prisăcani village is a historical monument with the code IS-II-m-B- 04231 and was built in 1737 (according to LMI) or 1797-1804 (according to research undertaken by Fr. Iulian Vornicu). Completion the works were carried out during his reign Veniamin Costachi. The Church, dedicated to Saints Michael and Gabriel, keeps the plan of the Christian church, formed from altar in circular form, narthex, porch and bell tower raised above Its. The entrance to the narthex is made through a wooden door, above which we read the inscription: "This Holy Church built by Metropolitan Veniamin Costachi in 1804, it was repa-rat and tied with iron and was painted after a the great mouth of the Prut river in 1928 (...)". The painting of the church was made by the artist Ilie Ionescu in 1928, which attempted to reproduce the original Byzantine painting.

Location

Prisăcani, Prisăcani village
