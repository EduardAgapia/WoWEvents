Church of the Holy Archangels Michael and Gabriel

Description

It was built between 1985 and 1993, on a land located in the center of the village, donated by four Christian families. The church is built of brick, having the shape of ship, respecting the Moldovan style of construction. The edifice presents two spires, one of which is a bell tower, and a porch open. The iconostasis of the church was brought in the year 1913 from Golia Monastery and includes Painted icons in the XIX century. The interior painting was made between 2002 and 2007, in the "al fresco" technique by painters church Magda and Robert Cârciumaru.

Location

Prisăcani, Prisăcani village
