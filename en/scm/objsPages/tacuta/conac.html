Sturdza Mansion from Tăcuta

Description

Ensemble of Sturdza Mansion Located in se area of the silent village, with the number registered in the List Historical Monuments: VS-II-a-B-06888; The settlement is built in the village, on the central road of the settlement. To the south of the monastery are the hills Chetreștilor and Rădueştilor. In the XVII – XX centuries, the village came to the possession of May many owners: Dumitrache Chiriţă, mare postelnic (1610); Dumitru Buhuş, fiancée III (1632); Ioniţă Canta, postelnic (1790); Ioniță Palade, aga (1810); the Sturza family (1824); Samuel and Mayer Veissengrun, bankers (1882); General N. Racoviţă/Drossu family (1886); the Dobrescu family (1891-1949).

Location

Tăcuta, Tăcuta village
