"Saint Emperors Constantine and Helen" Church

Description

The new church was built in 1894. According to the inscription, it was founded by the owners of the estate, Costache and Amalia Pancescu. Their graves are in the churchyard. The church is built of brick, on a stone foundation and is covered with tin. The construction has the shape of a ship, in style Moldovan. Inside, it is painted by Nicholas Dimitriu, the iconostasis being made by the same artist, made of linden wood. Important repair work were f in 1950 and 1984.
