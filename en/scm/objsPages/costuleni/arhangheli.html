”Holy Archangels” Church

Description

The first church dedicated to The Holy Princes Michael and Gavriil from Cozia village, was built of beams wooden in 1796 (other sources indicate 1813-15, or 1817) by the boyar Răducanu Roset. According to other sources, it was built either at the end of eighteenth century (1786), either at the beginning of xix century (1813 or 1817). In 1922 it was decided to build a new church, from the wall, and the old wooden church was donated to the village Coropceni. Construction lasted until 1957, when it was painted and sanctified. The church was rehabilitated in years 2010-2012, when the roof was changed to have a Stefanian style.

Location

Costuleni, Cozia village
