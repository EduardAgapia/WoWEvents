Eiffel bridge from Ungheni

Description

The project of this railway link between Chisinau – Ungheni – Iaşi was completed on August 14th 1869, during the bilateral meeting between the Tsar Alexander II and Prince Charles I, who had place on the occasion of the visit of the Romanian ruler in Crimea; but he had been in the discussion since the time of of Alexandru Ioan Cuza. The line was completed between 1871 and 1876 by Lidkowski company on the Russian side and the first private railway construction contractor from Romania, led by the young engineer Grigore Heliad. Initially there was a wooden bridge over the Prut River, but it was taken by the waters following a Flood.

Location

Ungheni, Ungheni village
