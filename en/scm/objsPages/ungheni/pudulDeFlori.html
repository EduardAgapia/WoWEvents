The Flower bridge

Description

The Flower Bridge is the name of the actions that took place on the banks of the Prut River in the early 1990s respectively 1991, and then in 2015. On this occasion, for the first time since the Second War worldwide, the Romanian-Soviet border could be crossed without documents. The first Bridge of Flowers took place on May 6, 1990, when the inhabitants of Romania were allowed as on the day of the respectively, between 13:00 and 19:00, to cross the Prut river into Soviet Moldova without a passport and visa. At the event was attended by about 1,200,000 people from both banks of the Prut River at the 8 border crossing points between Romania and Soviet Moldova. Symbolically, to these events were launched flowers on the water of the Prut, on both banks and on bridges.

Location

Ungheni, Ungheni village
