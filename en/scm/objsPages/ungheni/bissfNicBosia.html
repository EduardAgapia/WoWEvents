Saint Nicholas Church

Description

The church was dedicated to "St. Nicholas" and "St. Arch. Michael and Gabriel" was built in 1858. Is founded by the leaseholder of the estate, Ilie Paminian, Greek at origin. It built the church on behalf of debt to the administration of the Monastery of St. Sava. The same data are mentioned in the inscription, written in Greek. The edifice is built of brick, stone, concrete, with a roof in the shape of a roof framing made of galvanized sheet. In 1913 it is built the bell tower. The interior has no painting Mural. The iconostasis is made of linden wood, with icons painted in oil, dating from the construction Church.

Location

Ungheni, Bosia village
