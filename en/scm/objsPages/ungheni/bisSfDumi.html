Saint Demetrius Church

Description

The church dedicated to "St. Demetrius" was built in 1830. Before that, there was a church that was built in the XVIII century. Due to the subsidence of the land, the church was rebuilt, this time smaller. The church is built in the shape of a ship, on Basilical plan made of wood, stone and is covered with galvanized sheet. One of the bells was donated in 1914 by Gheorghe Pantea. On the inside it does not have mural painting. The iconostasis is carved in oak, fir and linden. It is said about the icons the iconostasis that they were brought from Russia (possibly due to the Slavic-Byzantine style). Over time has undergone several repair works, among which one capital between 1896-1906.
