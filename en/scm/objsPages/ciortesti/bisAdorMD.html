”Assumption of Mary” Church

Description

”Assumption of Mary” Church from the village Coropceni was founded by Ioan Beceriu and his wife its Catherine. It was built of brick and stone, shaped like a ship, with a bell tower above the narthex, with a two-water roof, and rounded above the Holy Shrine. It was finished and consecrated on February 11, 1846. Along time the church underwent numerous repairs, respectively seven, between 1900 and 1991. In the year In 1994, extensive consolidation works have begun, endowment and fencing of the church, works completed in 2010.

Location

Ciortești, Coropceni village
