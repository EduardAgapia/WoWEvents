Miclești Forest

Description

The Micleşti Forest, partially located on the territory of the Ciorteşti commune, is a site of Avifaunistic importance with the code ROSPA0096, established in 2007. With an area of 8631 hectares, the site is part of integral of the European ecological network Natura 2000, in particular due to the conditions of feeding, nesting and living for sedentary, transitory or migratory bird species. This is favored by the predominant oak forest but also the nearby agricultural areas, ideal places for procuring food, especially for Aquila Heliaca, a globally threatened eagle species. The great distance from the cities and the presence of the human footprint in a small manner created the premises for a high biodiversity development in this less known area but with potentials tourist valences.

Location

Ciortești
