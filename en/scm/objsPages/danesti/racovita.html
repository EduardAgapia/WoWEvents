Emil Racoviță Memorial House

Description

The mansion where the Memorial House "Emil" is located Racovita" is located in the eponymous village of Dăneşti commune, which bears the name of the well-known scientist and speleologist. It is part of a ensemble of historical monuments (code VS-II-a-B- 06795), comprising the old body (VS-II-a-B- 06795.01), new body (VS-II-a-B-06795.02), the ruins of the cellars (VS-II-a-B-06795.03) and the park with secular trees (VS-II-a-B-06795.04). Mansion dates from the nineteenth century, being erected by to Emil Racovita's parents, Gheorghe and Euphrosyne, on an estate of 1000 ha of arable land and 500 ha of forest.

Location

Dănești, Emil Racoviță village
