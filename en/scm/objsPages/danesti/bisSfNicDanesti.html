Saint Nicholas Church

Description

The church dedicated to "St. Nicholas" from Dăneşti village it was built in 1935, of brick pressed and without plaster, having the form of sailing ship. On the site of this church there was a church of valătuci, built by the community to the rebels, around 1780. In the synod The current church is written that all the expense for the building of the church was borne by the village community and several institutions. The Church has was consecrated on November 21, 1935 by the PS Bishop Nifon Criveanu of the Diocese of Husi, together with a group of 21 priests. In in 2009, several works of consolidation and painting of the interior walls were carried out, completed at the end of the same year.

Location

Dănești, Dănești village
