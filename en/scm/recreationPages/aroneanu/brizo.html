Brizo Beach, Sports & Lounge

Description

Brizo Sports Base sums up a total area of over 10,000 sqm arranged at the most High standards that offer a mix of activities, amusement, the possibility of renting beach courts volleyball and water sports, in one of the most beautiful places in Romania. The location includes:
– 1 beach volleyball arena equipped with stands;
– 2 beach volleyball courts;
– 1 port with boat launch pad;
– 1 esplanade of 200 linear meters with hot water showers every 20 m;
– 1 rental center and shop for sports goods and equipment;
– 5 DOM-type structures with a diameter of 8 m, 12 m and 16 m;
– sandy beach with a capacity of 100 premium sunbeds;
– relaxation areas, swimming pool;
– restaurant with 3 terrace areas, bar & lounge;
– Parking arranged with over 500 seats
