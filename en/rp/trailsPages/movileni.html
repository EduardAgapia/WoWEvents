Movileni Trail

Description

It is a circular route dedicated to walks through nature, culture and local history measuring approximately 34 km, crossing the villages Potângeni, Movileni, Larga Jijia and Rabbini. The route consists mostly of communal roads, arranged, but also undeveloped, of agricultural holding, a small part intersecting DJ 282, between the intersection of the Rabbini-Ursoaia road, up to Larga Jijia.

Trail length: 34 km
The route goes through the following points:

START - Aura Essential lavender field from Movileni.
The Round Mound - viewpoint.
Saint Spiridon Church in Potângeni.
Saint Spiridon Church in Movileni.
Movileni City hall.
Movileni halt.
Monument and cemetery of Soviet soldiers from Larga Jijia.
The Church of the Assumption of the Virgin Mary in Rabbini.
The monument of the Romanian heroes from Rabbini-Ursoaia.
Larga-Jijia Railway Station.
Moldova Delta Complex.
