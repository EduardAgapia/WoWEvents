Valea Lupului Trail

Description

It is a circular route dedicated to walks through nature, culture and local history measuring approximately 23 km, bringing together 13 points of interest, which have been documented and selected for their cultural value, natural and gastronomic, being considered representative of the area. The route consists mostly of communal roads, arranged, but also of undeveloped roads, of agricultural holding. In point no. 7. Valea Prisăcii, intersects with the route QR4ALL - Rediu.

Trail length: 23 km
The route goes through the following points:

START/Finish - City Hall
Public park.
Nature Reserve - Secular meadows.
Uric Hill - viewpoint.
Tăuteșilor valley - flora and fauna.
Sloping slide.
Prisăcii Valley - viewpoint and common point with QR4ALL - Rediu Route.
Church of the Holy Emperors on the Beldiman Plateau.
Popas Păcurari - restaurant.
Padre Pio Catholic Church.
"La Colțul Dulce Sărat" - gastronomic point.
Secondary School ”Prof. Mihai Dumitriu”.
Church of the Descent of the Holy Spirit.
