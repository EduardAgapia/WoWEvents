Șipote Trail

Description

It is a circular route dedicated to walks through nature, culture and local history measuring approximately 42 km, crossing the villages Şipote, Chişcăreni, Iazul Vechi, passes on the bank of the accumulation from Hălceni, Hălceni, MItoc, Iazul Nou, Şipote Hospital, located at the territorial border between Botosani and Iasi counties. The route consists mostly of communal roads, landscaped, but also undeveloped roads, of agricultural holding, part of the route intersecting DJ 282, from Mitoc to the Hospital at Camp-Şipote.

Trail length: 42 km
The route goes through the following points:

The Church of Luca Arbore, the doorkeeper of Suceava.
The Church of the Nativity of the Virgin Mary in Chişcăreni.
Church of Saint Anthony of Padua in the Old Pond.
Viewpoint towards Jijia and Miletin Ponds.
Water accumulation from Hălceni.
Saint George Church in Hălceni.
Secluded place with wildlife.
Church of the Assumption in Mitoc.
The Church of the Assumption Saint Parascheva Church in Iazu Nou.i Maicii Domnului in Mitoc.
Camp-Şipote Hospital - camp and cemetery of prisoners of World War I.
The monument to Soviet soldiers in Şipote.
Şipote Village Library and Museum.
Şipote City Hall.
