Plugari Trail

Description

It is a circular route dedicated to walks through nature, culture and local history measuring approximately 31 km, crossing the villages Plugari, Oneşti, Borosoaia and Miletin, the last village belonging to Prajeni commune. located in Botosani County. The route consists mostly of communal roads, landscaped, but also undeveloped roads, of agricultural holding, part of the route intersecting DJ 282 B, from the intersection to Miletin to Plugari.

Trail length: 31 km
The route goes through the following points:

Plugari Stadium.
Agrofruct Farm Plugari.
Coroleuca Forest.
Saint Demetrius of Borosoaia Church.
Miletin Village.
Cotul Bicului Pond.
Imaşul Plugari.
Plugari Cultural home.
Church of the Holy Emperors of Plugari.
Plugari City Hall.

Location
