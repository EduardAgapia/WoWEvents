The vents of the tunnel - Halta Movileni

Description

The construction of the movileni halt tunnel began in 1890 and was completed for six years later, being realized according to the plans of the architect Ioan Cantacuzino, descendant of the sea boyar families. It was erected in order to serve the route Dorohoi - Iasi, one of the the first railway lines built in the XIX century in our country. In the early 90's it was started a process of consolidation of the tunnel, but out of the total length of 964 m were only a hundred were restored, and the consolidation was abandoned due to lack of funds. The tunnel has two ventilation chimneys, built of brick brought from the factory in Ciurea.

Location

Românești, Halta Movileni
