The salts on the Ileana Valley

Description

The salts of the Ileana Valley are a site of community importance (SCI) and are declared Natura 2000 site in 2007 with the code ROSCI0221. The site overlaps the territory administrative division of the communes of Dumeşti and Româneşti, Iaşi County, with an area of 112 Ha. Within the site there is the natural reserve of botanical interest (IUCN category IV) "The salts on the Ilena Valley" with an area of 5.90 ha. Surface of the protected natural area it overlaps the catchment area of the Ileana River (14 km - tributary of the Bahlui River). It is located at an altitude varying between 40 meters and 100 meters, in a floor of vegetation specific to the central eastern part of the Moldavian Platform, respectively the floor of forest-steppe. The predominant vegetation is one of tall hygrophilic grasses, having a diversity of diversity remarkable floristic and vegetable.

Location

Dumești and Românești
