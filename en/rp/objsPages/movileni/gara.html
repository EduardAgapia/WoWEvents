Larga Jijia Train station

Description

Larga Jijia Railway Station is one of the monumental buildings built at the end of the XIX century along the railways of Moldova. The Ia»ôi - Dorohoi line, on which this railway station is located, was built between 1891 and 1896 and put into service on August 1, 1896. Just like other stations impressive on this route, the Larga Jijia railway station was built by the "Concession" engineer Ioan G. Cantacuzino" after the studies and projects elaborated under the direction of engineer Elie Radu.The massiveness of the unique volume and the central part discreetly embossed in rezalit it reminds of the proportions of the Mogosoaia palace. Brick of Ciurea, used for the construction of the most beautiful railway stations in the old Kingdom (Moldavia and Wallachia) and left apparent, helps to valuing them by the nobility and vigor of the material, namely by the quality exceptional of the Ciurea clay. Thus, this station fits perfectly into the national style of railway architecture through expressiveness and minerality.
