Church of St. Spiridon – Movileni

Description

The first church in Movileni village was built from the wall between 1804-1805 by Florea Baş - Ceaus, of Greek descent. Over time she suffered some damage, so in 1909 it was restored, but during the First World War the church burned almost entirely. Church ''St. Spiridon'' from Movileni village was built between 1920-1927 in the same place. Stone and the necessary brick was bought from the demolition of some old inns in the village of Mălăeşti. The church is built with stone walls up to the window, and the top of the brick. Is covered with tin, and in the western part it has a tower that also serves as a bell tower.

Location

Movileni, Movileni village
