Church of the Holy Great Martyr Demetrius

Description

The church being dedicated to "The Holy Great Martyr Dumitru" and the parish with the same name were erected in 1937, on the site of the former church, built at the initiative of Gheorghe Asachi. It raised old stone church with the help of the clăcaşilor of on his estate, during the holidays, and Sundays. Even so, the new construction was done without the consent of the Metropolia. According to Fr. Parish Protosyngellos Sophrony Ungureanu, when The metropolitan passed through the village and discovered that a church had been built without his will and cursed it never to be served in it. Mihai A. Dascălu, chapel at the current church, mentions another variant: "As I did not know when this church was built, so I do not know even when it shall be torn down."

Location

Prăjeni, Lupăria village
