Church of the Holy Archangels Michael and Gabriel

Description

The church dedicated to "The Holy Archangels Michael and Gabriel" was built in June 1966. Construction it lasted only two weeks. At that time, the communist regime forbade construction of holy places. Some of the objects owned by the church were donated, including: the iconostasis of the the part of the glassware parish, jud. Iaşi; cult books on behalf of the parish Frumuşica, jud. Botoşani. Over the next few years, a parish house, a feast house and a turnclopotnita with the same help of parishioners were also erected.

Location

Prăjeni, Miletin village
