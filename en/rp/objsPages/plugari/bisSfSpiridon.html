St. Spiridon Church

Description

The Church of St. Spiridon in the village of Oneşti dates back to the reign of Alexandru Moruzi, May exactly, since 1805. The church has a trifled shape, and the inscription above the entrance door is open to us presents the founders of the church (Costache Ghica and his wife Maria Cantacuzino). This is not painted, but has a valuable iconostasis painted in the first half of the XIX century. Is built of stone and brick walls with a thickness of 80-100 cm. Legend has it that the stone would have been brought from hand to hand from Deleni. The place of worship is a historical monument (IS-II-m-B-04209) and is classified as a wall church.

Location

Plugari, Onești village
