The Church of the Holy Voivodes Michael and Gabriel

Description

Forăști Parish was founded in 2000, the church place being consecrated three years later. In 2005, the parish receives as space the headquarters of the former Agricultural Production Cooperative, in order to it officiated liturgical services and to serve as a parish home. The works of raising the church with the patrons of the Holy Archangels Michael and Gabriel and the Resurrection of the Lord began in 2006 and were completed in 2009, with the help of parishioners and funds received from the commune Hall Gropnitsa. The church was consecrated on October 2, 2010, by His Eminence Teofan, Metropolitan of Moldavia and Bukovina. Currently, the parish priest of the Church of the Holy Voivodes of Forăşti is Father Marian Barbieru.

Location

Gropnița, Forăști village
