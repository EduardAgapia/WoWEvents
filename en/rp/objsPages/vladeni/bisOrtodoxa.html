Orthodox Church

Description

Construction of the Orthodox Church in the village of Broşteni it started with the year 2000, at the request of the parishioners to His Beatitude Patriarch Daniel. The land on which the church is located was donated by to Gheorghe and Elena Aramă, from the village Iacobeni and sanctified at the feast of the Annunciation of His Eminence Calinic. In 2013 they were started the plan and documentation of the edifice of cult, by architect Anca Elena Vasile. The plan and documentation of the church were drawn up in 2013, under the guidance of the architect. The painting was made by Sergiu Gheorghiță Vatamaniuc, after the painter's project Mihai Chiuaru, and the furniture and the iconostasis were made by the sculptor Ioan Hantar and the painter Cătălin Simiciuc.

Location

Vlădeni, Broșteni village
