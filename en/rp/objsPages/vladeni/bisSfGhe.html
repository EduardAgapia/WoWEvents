Saint George Church

Description

The first church in the village of Iacobeni has was founded after 1860, by Gheorghe and Zamfira Cernea, having the patron saint of St. George. In 1926, the church required repairs both in inside as well as outside, moment in which was also attached to the porch. Abode The cult of the present has a structure simple, with the ceiling of the wall semicircular and roof in framing in two waters. The iconostasis is made of wood fir tree with a sculpture in straight lines. The interior was painted between 2009 and 2012 and adorned with silver royal icons, with ornamented in relief.
