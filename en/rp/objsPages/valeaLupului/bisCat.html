Padre Pio Catholic CHurch

Description

Construction work on the Padre Pio au Catholic Church started in the spring of 2012 and five have been completed years later. Catholic community in Valea Lupului it has an old history, being composed of families that live there for a long time, but also from families that have come in the last years with the development of the urban area of Iasi. Originally the community belonged to the "Saint Anthony" Parish of Iaşi, but in 2007 when Alois Hîrja was appointed priest, His Grace Aurel Percă inaugurated the chapel "Saint Saint Padre Pio", the faithful wanting as the patron of the community Catholic to be Saint Pius of Pietrelcina.

Location

Valea Lupului
