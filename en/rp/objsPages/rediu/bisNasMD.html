The Church of the Nativity of the Mother of God

Description

The parish "Nativity of the Mother of God" has was founded in 1996, when the elders village along with war veterans submitted a request to the Archdiocese Iasi. Motivation was the road of walked to the church in the village of Rediu, as well as the need for a village church and to a priest who is constantly present. Thus, in 1996, the works of construction. In 2001, in a church on half lifted and bare on the inside was officiated the first service. In the same year it was bought a plot of land adjacent to the church, where the breazu village cemetery was arranged.
