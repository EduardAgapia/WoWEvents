The forest and the meadows of Mârzeşti

Description

The forest and meadows from Mârzești represent a site of community importance, declared a site Natura 2000 in 2007, with the code ROSCI0171. It has an area of about 200 ha, being at an average altitude of about 125 meters, being located on the territory administrative of the communes of Popricani and Rediu. The importance of the site lies in the presence of steppe meadows and forest-steppe forests Siberian that preserves a series of Natural habitats of the type: Meadows and pannonian salt marshes and ponto-sarmatic, Stepe ponto-sarmatic, Meadows of low altitude (Alopecurus pratensis, Sanguisorba officinalis), Deciduous bushes ponto-sarmatic and vegetation of Eurosiberian forest-steppe with Quercus spp and protects flora and fauna specific to the Plateau Moldovan.

Location

Popricani and Rediu communes
