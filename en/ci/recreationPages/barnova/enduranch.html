Enduranch

Description

Enduranch is a natural and off-road adventure park in North-East Romania, i.e. in wild east. The services offered are:
- car tests / moto / ATV / bicycles / trucks / any motorized vehicles or non-motorized, to test new models, new pilots, new skills.
- The motocross circuit in Moldova built has a surface "hard-pack", a length of 1,300 meters
- team-buildings organized away from the unleashed world and yet close to city (15 km from the center of Iasi).
- place for barbecue - access to specially arranged places, in the middle of nature
- outdoor events
- camping place - access to camping places specially arranged for tents or caravans and wooden cottages.

Location

Bârnova, Pietrărie village
