Adventure Park HAMAK

Description

Hamak adventure park is one of the attractions of Iasi, especially in the warm season. Here is arranged a whole complex where activities are made available varied for both children and adults. From zip line, hiking, paintball, riding, sport fishing or hauling in the hammock to pontoons and tables placed variously where you can drink or eat something. The five routes arranged ascending according to the level of the brave constitute the main magnet of the park. Of these two are for the little ones, and the rest for the big ones, with varying degrees of difficulty.
It is the first adventure park opened in Iasi County, being conceived as an area of leisure. Also, festivals take place here, but you also have the opportunity to organize your own events in indoor or outdoor spaces, in the open air.

Location

Bârnova, Pietrărie village
