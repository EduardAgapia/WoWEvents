”The Spring of Healing” Church

Description

The actions of building the church started on December 24, 1979 (cf. receipt) with bringing a 200 kg bell by commuter employees from the village, which was installed for the beginning in the household of Mr. Epitrop Moraru Gh. Vasile. From there this the bell first rang in 1980 at the funeral of the elder Bors D. Gheorghe.
Then an old house was bought by Mr. Epitrop Moraru Gh. Vasile from Mr. Bors V. Constantin (d. 2006) , on the 10th. 02. 1980 (cf. receipt) . This Old house was divided to serve the priest in any time and was installed the bell tower in the courtyard of the chapel. The situation continued until the spring of 1981, when the village community decided building a Holy Church according to the needs of the village (150 families) under the leadership of the parish priest (cf. the community statement of 18. 01. 1980) The Court of First Instance John Zechariah. The works for this new church started in the spring of the year 1982, behind the old chapel.

Location

Țibănești, Războieni village
