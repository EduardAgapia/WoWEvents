Pios Parascheva Church

Description

Glodenii Gandului village is part of Țibăneşti commune and is located at a distance of 45 kilometers from the city of Iasi. It's a fairly new settlement of about. 130 years, the name of the village being taken from the first man who built a household settlement here, namely Glodeanu. He was called Glodenii Gandului to distinguish himself from the village Glodeni from Negresti – Vaslui area.
The foundation stone of the village church was put at the initiative of Fr. Alexandru Popa and of a group of householders, with the approval of His Eminence Bishop Teofil Herineanu in 1949. The village was the daughter of the Țibănești parish and was part at that time of the Episcopate Novel and Husi.

Location

Țibănești, Glodenii Gândului village
