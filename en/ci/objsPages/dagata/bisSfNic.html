Saint Nicholas Church

Description

The construction of the church began in 1925 the help of the parishioners of the parishioners of Dagâța up to the level of the windows, then the covering, painting and endowment of the monastery being made by the former owner of the old estate Dagâța, the boyar Nicolae Tăutu. The painting of the church has it was carried out in neo-Byzantine style by the painter Nicolae Cojomenco from Chernivtsi, and the sculpture made by Iorgu Săpătorul from Roman.
The consecration of the church was on September 8, 1938 by His Grace Ilarion Băcăuanul of the Diocese of Roman and re-consecrated on June 3, 2007 by His Beatitude Patriarch Daniel.

Location

Dagâța, Dagâța village
