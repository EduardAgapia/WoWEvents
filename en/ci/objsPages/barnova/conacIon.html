Ion Inculet's Mansion

Description

Ion Inculet's mansion is located in the village of Bârnova, Iaşi County and belonged to the family politician Ion Inculet (1884-1940). This edifice is located at a distance of about 6 kilometers from the Three Cabbage Rolls Inn in Iasi. The mansion was rebuilt in the year 2008, to house a social center of the MetropolitanAte of Moldavia and Bucovina. Current the building no longer resembles only in part the old mansion.
In 2002, the mansion from Bârnova was donated to the MetropolitanAte of Moldavia and Bucovina by the engineer Ion I. Inculet. In 2008, the mansion was demolished, being rebuilt in a style that reminds only vaguely of the previous edifice. The masonry of the old mansion was preserved, being recessed in the new building built. The mansion was added one floor and several other rooms on the the back side, which changed the plane of the building.

Location

Bârnova, Bârnova village
