Ethnographic Museum "Garage with Memories"

Description

The ethnographic museum "Garage with Memories" is located in Pietrărie village, Bârnova commune, not one km from the main road, on the left-hand side of the direction of travel, 500 m away from the Repedea Paleontological Reserve and 2 km away from the Monastery Bârnova (historical monument, construction from the XVIIIth century).
In the two rooms of the "Garage with Memories", the objects are displayed according to of the material from which they were made or intended, either on the shelves or on the Floors. There are ceramic objects whose age varies between 20 and 200 years, objects made of various braids of rushes, corn husks, twigs (e.g. baskets for the market, straw hats), textiles, used objects in housekeeping, wooden objects (e.g. small liquid storage vessels, the so-called "baskets" means small tools used in the household, tools used in woodworking, small objects used for agricultural purposes.

Location

Bârnova, Pietrăria village
