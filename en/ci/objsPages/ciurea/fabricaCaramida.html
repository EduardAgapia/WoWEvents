Brick factory

Description

It was founded in 1891 by Gheorghe Duca, today being a patrimony keeper valuable industrial and technical. After its opening, all buildings for depot, passengers, annex buildings on the line Vaslui-Iași, Iaşi-Dorohoi, Bucharest-Constanța, from Râmnicul Sărat as well as many railway bridges were idificiate only of red brick of Ciurea. Currently, the brick factory makes part of the company "Pheonix SA", private company with object of activity producing metalworking, mechanical processing, textiles for the internal market, and export.
The main building with the apparent brick façade is provided inside with a durable wooden structure. It is worth noting that it is still preserved, although not works, a watch "Paul Garnier" manufactured in Paris and mounted on the façade of the building . The building houses the clay homogenization and shredding plant, the pressing, drying space and two Hoffmann-type furnaces for burning bricks.

Location

Ciurea, Lunca Cetățuii village
