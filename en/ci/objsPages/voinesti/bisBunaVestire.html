CHURCH OF THE ANNUNCIATION

Description

The church from Vocoteşti village is dedicated to the "Annunciation" and is placed on the plateau of North-East of the village, at the edge of the county road, which crosses the eastern part of the Voineşti commune and connects through the west with the village Mânjeşti, Budeşti and with the village Hadâmbu, both belonging to the commune of Mogoşeşti. The church is built around the years 1930-1940 by the parishioners of the village in the lead with Gheorghe Iepure, singer of the church, buried near the church in 1948. The "Annunciation" Church in Vocoteşti does not have a mural, as it it is built of wood and earth, lined both inside and outside with planks, painted in oil.

Location

Voinești, Vocotești village
