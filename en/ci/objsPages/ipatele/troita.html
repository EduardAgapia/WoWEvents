The cross dedicated to the Mother of God

Description

It was raised in honor of the Mother of God, in remembrance of the miracle that The Most Holy Mother of God committed her at Ipatele on June 10, 2015, when, in following the prayers within the Mystery of the Holy Unction, God poured out rain over the thirsty earth. Then, the miracle-working icon of the Mother of God from the Monastery Hadâmbu was brought in procession in this locality, with the blessing of HE Teofan, Metropolitan of Moldavia and Bucovina, through the love and goodwill of Archimandrite Nicodemus Gheorghiţă, the abbot of hadâmbu monastery.

Location

Ipatele, Ipatele village
