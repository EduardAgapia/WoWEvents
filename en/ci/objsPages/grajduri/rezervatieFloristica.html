”Poiana cu Schit” Floristic reserve

Description

”Poiana cu Schit” is a protected area of national interest that corresponds to category IV IUCN (floristic nature reserve) located in Iasi County, on the territory administrative administration of grajduri commune. The natural area is located in the Moldavian Plateau, in the south of Iaşi County, on the banks of the Cocoarei brook (tributary of the Rebricea River) in the Boroşeşti forest, in the western territory of poiana cu cetate village.
