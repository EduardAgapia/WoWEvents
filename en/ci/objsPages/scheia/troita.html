The cross "Cișmeaua Iacob"

Description

Scheia, an oasis of tranquility and peace, with a quince and must aroma that arouses our memories from times long gone, where stories about the people of the place were born. About the villagers funny poems were written by the intellectuals of the village with that sense of humor, with crazy characters, full of optimism, all this makes you dream of childhood and to the beauty of the place. At the entrance to the village you are greeted by a cross, a symbol of worship Christian built of stone.
Mircea Eliade assimilates the cross with the pillar of the sky, the cosmic axis that once ascended make the space habitable. The village needs divine protection, not being provided with a door, the space has the porch open to the world and to every traveler, who can worship the icon painted in Byzantine style on the stone slab, with the theme "The Spring healing." The freshwater spring was brought to the surface by stonemason craftsmen, people of the place, many years ago. After decades of waiting, it was painted icon by Aurelia Carabineru, daughter of the village returned to her native lands.

Location
