Church of Saints Joachim and Anna

Description

The holy place was built in a relatively short time, the works starting in the year 2008, the parish being founded in 1996 by detaching from the mother-parish "Saint Nicolae"-Galata from mogoşeşti commune. The church has an architecture in the form of a ship, having the length of 21 m and the width of 6.5 m, and the bell tower has a height of 10 m. The works were carried out by Fr. Dumitru-Cristinel Puiu, priest since 1996 at the Parish "St. Nicholas" - Galata from Mogoşeşti. Church "Saints Joachim and Anna" of the Parish Şanta-Mogoşeşti, Protopopiatul 3 Iaşi, Iaşi County, was consecrated on Sunday, the 16th October 2016, by His Grace Calinic Botoşăneanul.

Location

Mogoșești, Mogoșești-Șanta village
