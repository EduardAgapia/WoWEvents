„St. Varvara” Church

Description

The building of the church as started in 1828 and ended in 1832 and the material used it was brick. The church is dedicated to Saint Varvara, which is celebrated on the 4th December.
In the year of completion, the church was consecrated, endowed with icons iconostasis, which it was painted with red paint and the necessary vestments were donated to the services. All these were accomplished by the stadania of John Beldiman, the then committed tenant of the estate, and the founder of the church. He is buried in the place of prayer, which he built. In 1873 the church was repaired by Constantin Rusovici by "his vill and expense".

Location

Mogoșești, Mogoșești – Frumoasa village
