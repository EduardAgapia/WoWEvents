Village Museum

Description

The museum is a settlement of culture and spirituality where the village life is reflected over time, through its unique, authentic traditions and customs. Exhibits its are presented in three rooms placed as follows: - in the first room, also called The "living room" is found the "dowry" of a peasant family and a corner dedicated to the poet Mihai Eminescu; - in the second room are presented "household" objects usual for life village daily newspapers, from the windows with "documents" attesting the existence of members of a peasant families, but also a special numismatic "corner"; - in the third room you can see the "place" where the family gathers and the story about the necessary daily living, but and the place where memories were overcome. The room is provided with a intended "corner" The deity and one dedicated to the poet Grigire Vieru. Guests can rest at the table friendship in the yard full of flowers, fruit trees, near the mo cross built in the 90s.
