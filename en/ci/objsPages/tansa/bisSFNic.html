Saint Nicholas Church

Description

Saint Nicholas Church, dedicated to the "Holy Trinity", from Tansa parish, Iasi county, has was built between 1903-1918, built of carved stone blocks from its foundation to the roof, by the parishioners of this parish. The church is built with a lot of artistic taste, especially the spire that is located in the western part of the church and serves as a bell tower. in the courtyard of the Church can be visited the monument Heroes' Cross. The painting was made by the priest Damian from Bucharest in 1918, when it was also sanctified.

Location

Tansa, Tansa village
