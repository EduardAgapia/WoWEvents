Wooden church "St. Arch. Michael and Gabriel"

Description

The wooden church "St. Voivodes" dating from 1780 is an architectural monument of local interest, located in the village of Suhuleț.
The church was built in 1774 by the inhabitants of this village, of oak wood on the foundation of stone (by Tansa), originally covered with shingles. In 1985 it was covered with sheet metal, and the walls cement. Over time, this church was renovated by the priest Vasile Conoro and restored by the priest Gheorghe Barnovschi. The church was never painted, being adorned only with icons of saints and royal feasts. Iconostasis dates back to 1774, the painting of icons being executed in the style of the century alXVIII th.

Location

Tansa, Suhuleț village
