Village Museum

Description

The museum manages to outline the life of yesteryear, the village life not yet touched by the haste Time. The house with an age of over 100 years in which the museum operates was rehabilitated in a traditional manner. Tansians knew how to give color to the whole village, while realizing in almost any aspect of daily life, elements of art decorative to please the eye.
Tansa ceramics is also part of the patrimony of the Ethnographic Museum of Moldova in within the National Museum Complex "Moldova" Iasi. Between December 11, 2020 and June 1, 2021, at the Palace of Culture in Iasi, it is can visit the temporary exhibition "Ceramica de Tansa".

Location

Tansa, Tansa village
