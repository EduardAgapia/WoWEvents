"Saint George" Church

Description

The parish church from Drăguşeni village is founded by the Rășcanu family and is built of brick, in the shape of a ship, with a tower in which the bells are located. Year the exact of its construction is not known. There are two inscribed dates in two places different from the church: March 28, 1815, 1883 and a recorded testimony of an old man who said that August T. Rășcanu (founder buried in the nave of the church) would be bought, around the 1880s, the land with all the church. We know for sure, however, that the current edifice was built with the help of August Rășcanu at the priest's perseverance Teodor Cibotaru and the epitropes Vasile Gâdilaș and Grigorie Apostu.
The iconostasis of the drăguşeni church is of great value being painted in style Byzantine by the Greek painter Mihailis Vasilis. In the altar we have another iconostasis, with feast icons and important saints from the church calendar, of a value and an invaluable beauty. In the patrimony of the church there is also a filigrammed Holy Chalice from 1847, a bell with an indecipherable Slavonic inscription and a large number of ritual books of the XIX century.

Location

Drăgușeni, Drăgușeni village
