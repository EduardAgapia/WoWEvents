„Holy Trinity” Church

Description

The parish "Holy Trinity" is located in Frenciugi Village, Drăguşeni Commune - Protopopiatul III Iaşi.
The old church of the village is built of braiding of elm twigs, on Stone foundation, beaten on both sides with veils and covered with galvanized sheet. Viewed on the outside it has a simple appearance, and in 1890 the exterior walls were lined with fir plank, painted in blue oil. It was repaired in 1960 by priest Constantin Popescu. The church is not painted . The iconostasis is painted in style Moldovan, but it is not carved, the painter is not known.
The inscription at the entrance to the church includes the following: "The Most Holy Cross is the patron saint of this church, whose foundation was made at the expense of the honorable Alexander Rîșcanu, biv vel paharnic for the forgiveness of his sins and that of his wife Casandra and a cocoons, George and Maria, for remembrance forever and ever. Amen. 1781"

Location

Drăgușeni, Frenciugi village
