The Church of the Holy Voivodes

Description

It is dedicated to "The Holy Voivodes Michael and Gabriel", it is a historical monument from the time of Socialist Republic of Romania. The church was built by the community of believers to that of that village in 1774, as is apparent from the inscription carved in wood on a beam above the entrance door to the church.
The church is built of oak wood on a cross-shaped stone foundation. From the beginning this church was covered with shingles. In the course of time it was repaired several times. A general repair was made by the former owner I. Stroie with his wife who also donated a body of land. In 1935 it was repaired radically by the former son of the village Fr. Petru Grigoriu from the Alexe Church in Bucharest, the son of the former Parish Priest. D. Grigoriu, devoted servant of this church. The architect and craftsmen are not known. The painting of the Church in the Byzantine style is smoked and darkened by the times. The church possesses books of worship with Cyrillic letters.

Location

Pâncești, Pâncești village
