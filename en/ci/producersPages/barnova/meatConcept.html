Meat Concept Store

Description

Meat Concept Store is a family business that was founded in 2014. First Open store was (and still is) located in the Păcurari area. Mission the business is to offer the iasi community a place where they can always find the same standard product quality, where customers are greeted by an extremely good team ready and always ready to help them in the shopping decisions they want to Make.
At the end of 2020 they opened a new Location, located in the vicinity of Iasi (in the village of Vişan from Bârnova commune) which sums up a specially dedicated production area carmangeries, a small restaurant dedicated to tasting events and events private, but also a traditional smoker, which smoked through a 100% natural process, using only beech or cherry wood.
In December 2021 they inaugurated a new brand that currently owns only one store, dedicated to smoked products, Horn – Grocery.

Location

Bârnova, Vișan village
