Description

"GRAMMA", a brand inspired by the power of wine, a concept designed to open a new direction in wine communication, that of intelligent conversation and language without blemish untied by the impact of liquors, because always the correct expression and harmonious represents the physical and mental balance. The name "Gramma" comes from the Greek old, where it meant letter, primordial unity by which words are performed on paper, to be shown and read.
The "Gramma Wines" winery is a construction in the heart of The Cherry Tree, Bârnova made after the model of the family vineyards in the French provence region. The labels are created by master Horațiu Mălăele, and in 2016 Gramma winery reached the Forbes USA Top "The coolest wine labels of spring 2016".

Location

Bârnova, Vișan village
