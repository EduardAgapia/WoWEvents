Mierea „Taină Vie”

Description

Honey „Taină Vie” ("Living Mystery") symbolizes the present testimony of traditional beekeeping of the most early times, of ancient counsels passed through generations, of the joy of living in a harmonious way with nature. Our grandparents conveyed our concern for beekeeping, thus developing the beekeeping farm "Taina Vie" from Bârnova commune, county Iasi.
„Taină Vie” is a source of health, honey being entirely the product bees, without additions of any kind.

Location

Bârnova, Bârnova village
