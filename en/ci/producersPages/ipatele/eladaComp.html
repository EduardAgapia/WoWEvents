ELADA COMPANY SRL

Description

S.C. ELADA - COMPANY S.R.L. has carried out grain cultivation activities, legumes, technical plants, seed production and marketing, execution mechanized of agricultural works, chemization and fertilization, provision of services to third parties, the marketing of their own products. Since its inception until now, the unit has carried out, in particular, activities for the cultivation of cereals and technical plants, in this year-on-year end with areas under wheat (approximately 624 ha per year), sunflower (about 362 ha), soya and maize (about 448 ha annually), rapeseed (about 223 ha) as well as permanent and temporary meadows (about 100 ha annually);
