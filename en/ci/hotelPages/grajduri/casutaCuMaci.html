„Căsuța cu maci” (House wih poppies) Pension

Description

„Căsuța Cu Maci” is built specifically to enliven the soul of every traveler that crosses its threshold. Traditional and colorful, the pension is adapted to the XXI century – thus uniting the useful with the pleasant. Pension in poiana cu cetate village consists of: a living room with a place to take the table, a fully equipped kitchen, a generous terrace and five double bedrooms with bathroom Private.

Location

Grajduri, Poiana cu Cetate village
