The Ancient Fortification from Moşna, point Cetățuia

Description

One kilometer from the village of Moşna, in the forest of north, on the hill hill, there is the Fortification Dacian from the Cetăţuia point. With an area of about 9 km, with an almost pentagonal shape, defended naturally and with the help of waves and deepened ditches, the fortification shows traces of habitation from the Dacian and predacic period. In this ceramic elements have been identified place bastarnic and cosiana amphorae demonstrating dwelling in the III-II centuries BC. In the perimeter of the fortress flint parts, objects of bronze and iron. The fortress from Moşna represents a place with a special symbolism for the community of Moşna. Cultural events, but also walks in nature are often associated with the fortification from Moşna, which he wants to highlight this lesser-known place. Several reenactments of battles have been carried out between Dacians and Romans with the help of the Geto-Dacian Cultural Association of Moldova.
