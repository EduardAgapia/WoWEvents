The Church of the Holy Voivodes

Description

The current church dedicated to "Saints Voivodes" is a historical monument (IS-II-m-B-04207) and was raised in two stages: the first between 1884-1890, and the second between 1890-1893. Before that there was another church, having the same patron, built around 1756. Of this there is only St. Stephen left. Table, which is located in the courtyard of the current church. The place of worship is built of burnt stone and brick, made by the locals, in the shape of a cross. At inside, the iconostasis is received in 1893, from His Eminence Metropolitan Iosif Naniescu. Iconostasis belonged to the Nicolae Domnesc Church in Iasi, in its turn received from Poland, brought by Antonie Ruset in 1676. It is carved in sycamore wood and painted in oil. It was restored in 1904, by the donation of King Carol I. The church underwent several repair and restoration works, among the most important in the years: 1901, 1937, 1984, 1997-2004.

Location

Moșna, Moșna village
