The Church of the Descent of the Holy Spirit

Description

By 1864 a place was given for the church (0.25 ha) but for 30 years nothing was built. In the year 1894 the foundation of a small wooden church was laid, brought from the village of Ghermăneşti. Here they supported themselves services until 1927, and the little church had the patron saint of St. Spiridon. In 1925 it was decided to build a new church with stone wall. In 1931 the place of worship was consecrated currently having two dedications: St. Spiridon and Sunday Descent of the Holy Spirit. Since 1980, the consolidation works have begun from the foundation to the roof, and on the walls of the church were made Byzantine paintings in raw fresco.

Location

Gorban, Gura Bohotin village
