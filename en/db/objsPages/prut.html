Prut River

Description

SCI protected natural area (site of community importance) Prut River has an area of 11861 protected ha, stretching on the territory of Iasi, Vaslui and Galați counties. This protected area it was established in 2007, being an integral part of the Natura 2000 network with the code ROSCI0213. Area the protected riverbed is located within the minor riverbed and the major riverbed of the Prut River. The presence of numerous habitats present on the site (mixed riparian forests, dystrophic and eutrophic lakes or natural grasslands of low altitudes) allowed the development of a rich diversity. Remarkable for this area is ichthyofauna, which is considered very rich. Being at the border with the Republic of Moldova - this area is deprived of the development of tourism activity Potential. The need for opinions from the border police makes access for recreational purposes very difficult. It is worth mentioning that over time there have been fools who have traveled with kayak the portion of the river from the entrance to Romania to the mouth of the Danube. This also brings benefits to this area natural, the anthropogenic impact being minimal, from this point of view. Within the Bohotin Hills LAG, this protected area overlaps the territory administrative of the communes of Grozeşti and Gorban.

Location

Grozești and Gorban communes
