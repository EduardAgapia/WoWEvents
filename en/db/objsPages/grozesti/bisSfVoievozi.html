The Church of the Holy Voivodes

Description

The Church of the Holy Voivodes, Colţu Cornii village in Grozeşti commune, Iaşi county, was built between the years 1941 - 1949, on the site of the old church about which it is said to have been set on fire on the day of the outbreak of the Second World War. Initially, the church was built of veils and Wooden beams with the help of the inhabitants and miller Vasile Zaverjinski. The objective was restored, observable elements being the roof, and construction of a specially made metal element for the bell tower. Currently, at the entrance of the church we notice an icon with St. Michael and Gabriel, the saints which is the patron saint of the church. New architectural elements are: the stone present at the windows of the church, extension of the roof at the entrance, which replaces the old marquise. The priest who serves this the church is Cristian - Valentin Irimia.

Location

Grozești, Colțu Cornii village
