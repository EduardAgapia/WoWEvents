Cozmești marching band

Description

''According to some, their music has its roots in the songs of military fanfare of the Ottoman Empire in the nineteenth century. But there is in the band's current repertoire and a important mass of authentic folk songs, such as The Bride's Song, Beaten or Serbian cousins. An important point could be in the year 1961, in Bucharest, when the band won Amateur Fanfare Festival. Same tournaments performed in France, the Netherlands, Germany and Belgium headlines the fanfare in Cozmesti as one of reference.'' (text taken from the REvista Satului magazine no. 15)
''The Fanfare from Cozmești was founded 120 years ago, being the oldest fanfare in the country, having deep roots in our tradition and culture, being the winner of numerous trophies and awards important at festivals and competitions, due to the technique of interpretation. The band's lead singer, Ina Chiriac, he has been collaborating with fanfare from Cozmești since the age of 14. ''(text taken from
