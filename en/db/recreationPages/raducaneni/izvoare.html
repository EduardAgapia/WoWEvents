Mineral springs from Răducăneni

Description

The healing properties of the springs from the Răducăneni forest were discovered in the first decades of the twentieth century, people then speaking of their "healing" role. It is said that in the period of interwar, some merchants sold this water "with the glass", the main customers being the Jews in the Răducăneni. The first chemical analyses on mineral waters from Răducăneni were made in the years 1928 and 1929 by Adrian Mayer and Radu Cernătescu of the University of Iași. It seems that since 1930 the first bottling of the mineral water from Răducăneni was also started. The 1930 study of Prof. Dr. A. Techari concludes that "this spring would be especially indicated in pulmonary asthma." Chemical studies and bacteriological performed even in the '60s-'70s revealed that the bicarbonated, sulfated water, calcium, magnesium and hypotonic from Răducăneni can treat gastrointestinal diseases, can be used in diuresis cures and for many other ailments.

Location

Răducăneni
